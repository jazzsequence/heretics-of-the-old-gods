
# Raven Queen

## What the Faithful Believe

Mortal turned goddess during the Divine Gate's establishment following the Calamity. Once a mortal mage who devised a way to cheat death and ascend to godhood — an act that drew the jealousy of another deity, resulting in the curse that claimed her name, memories, and mortal life. She became the Raven Queen: cold and calculating, obsessed with fate, determined to prevent others from artificially extending life or cheating death's natural order.

Governed her realm of Letherna within the [[Shadowfell]], sorting the souls of the dead and preventing undeath and the corruption of fate. Nemesis of [[Vecna]].

## Status After the Exodus

Departed during the Exodus alongside the other gods. Her absence left the [[Shadowfell]]'s soul-transit infrastructure — structural to the plane itself, predating her — operating without governance. A thousand years of unsorted soul transit has produced a Shadowfell dramatically more dangerous than anything the people of [[Nuxan]] understand.

The [[Echoes of the Raven]] worship her in secret and believe natural death sends souls to her care. They do not know what the Shadowfell has become in her absence.

## Tensions with Rival Faiths

The [[The Luxon|Luxon]]'s consecution prevents souls from entering the Shadowfell by binding them to beacons. The Raven Queen's followers view this as a violation of the natural order of death. The Luxon frames it as salvation from a now-dangerous plane, whether or not its leadership knows what the Shadowfell has actually become.
