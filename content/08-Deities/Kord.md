
# Kord

## What the Faithful Believe

God of strength, combat, and storms. One of the Prime Deities. His temple, the Trial Forge, is one of the founding institutions of [[Vasselheim]]. Faith centers on physical excellence and the honor of battle — proving oneself through conflict rather than through doctrine.

## Status After the Exodus

Departed with the other Prime Deities. The Trial Forge in Vasselheim persists. His worship has proven more resilient than many in the post-Exodus period because it asks little of its practitioners beyond personal excellence, making it difficult for the Luxon to suppress on purely theological grounds.
