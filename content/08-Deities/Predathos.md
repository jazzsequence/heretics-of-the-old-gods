
# Predathos

## Description

A Primordial entity predating the gods of Exandria — one of the first beings to exist in this reality, described as an "eater of gods." Not a deity itself and not worshipped by anyone; ranked by [[Vecna]]'s own file alongside Tharizdun as one of the two forces more powerful and threatening than any god. The gods feared and imprisoned it within the moon [[Ruidus]] before the Divergence, constructing the moon as a cage rather than a naturally-formed body.
