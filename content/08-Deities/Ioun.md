
# Ioun

## What the Faithful Believe

Goddess of knowledge, prophecy, and information. One of the Prime Deities. Patron of the [[Cobalt Soul]] monastic order — the Archive of the Knowing Mistress is named for her. Her faith centers on the free flow of information and the accumulation and preservation of knowledge as sacred acts.

## Status After the Exodus

Departed with the other Prime Deities. Her institutional legacy — the [[Cobalt Soul]] and its archives — survived longer than most divine institutions before being driven underground. The [[Cobalt Veil]] continues her core principle (truth must not be controlled) under a different name and by necessity covert methods.
