
# Bahamut

## What the Faithful Believe

God of justice, honor, and nobility. Patron of metallic dragons. One of the Prime Deities who fought against the Betrayer Gods during the Calamity. His temple, the Platinum Sanctuary, is one of the founding institutions of [[Vasselheim]] — the only major city to survive the Calamity intact.

## Status After the Exodus

Departed with the other Prime Deities. The Platinum Sanctuary in Vasselheim persists as a historical monument. His faith is among those considered heretical by [[The Luxon]]'s missionaries, though his worshippers generally represent the kind of institutional order that is harder to suppress than folk belief.
