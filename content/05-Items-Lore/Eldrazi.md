# Eldrazi

## Description

Colossal colorless entities from the Magic: The Gathering setting, incorporated into the Exandria Revisited campaign as planar invaders. Massive beings of abstract hunger and consumption with no desire beyond eating, reproducing, and destroying — no malice, just appetite at cosmological scale. They travel between planes, consuming them entirely before moving on.

Their ichor — residue left by Eldrazi presence — is referenced in the [[Children of the Hollow Moon]]'s founding alongside [[Ruidium]], suggesting both substances were discovered by the same mining operations in the ruins of the shattered [[Ruidus]].

**Exandria Revisited context:** The Eldrazi war is referenced in the campaign's setting as a past event that left scars on Exandria's planar fabric — the Astral scar referenced in the [[Whispered Rebellion]]'s goals (some members aim to resurrect [[Vecna]]'s fragment sealed in the Astral scar left by the Eldrazi war).
