
# Hellcatch Slang

## Description

A working glossary of regional slang used throughout the Hellcatch Valley and Bassuras.

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Wrench / Grease** | Tinkerer |
| **Kept** | Indentured by the Court. Sometimes refers to indenture by gangs. |
| **Wheel** | Crawler driver |
| **Runner** | (old, archaic) crawler driver — usually older drivers who specifically participated in the Deathwish Run |
| **Linebreaker** | Mostly used for drivers in raider bands in the Hellcatch, especially those who've survived driving through dust storms |
| **Ghost** | Someone who gets away (from Bassuras, from raiders, from storms) |
| **Crash** | Derogatory term for a driver (sometimes prophetic) |
