
# Consecution War

## Description

The religious purge initiated by [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]] across [[Tal'Dorei]], [[Issylra]], and [[Marquet]] in the Rising Action arc of HotOG. Framed as a divine mandate to unite Nuxan under the Luxon faith. The underlying logic: Caelestis believes a fully united world of faith would finally draw the attention of the Progenitors to protect Nuxan.

**Key events:**
- The [[Cerberus Assembly]] rebuilds the Volstrucker intelligence and military apparatus to support the Krynn advance
- [[Vasselheim]] is targeted specifically — to Caelestis it represents the historical heart of old-god heresy
- The [[Tal'Dorei Council]] organizes resistance and rebuilds the Emon Arcanum
- Resistance coalitions form in [[Issylra]] around old-god worshippers and clerical armies
- [[The Plank King]] exploits the chaos to annex the [[Menagerie Coast]]

**Resolution:** Caelestis is fatally wounded in a climactic battle. Her consecution attempt may or may not succeed. Her absence creates a power vacuum the Krynn Dynasty cannot fill — the lineage ends with her.
