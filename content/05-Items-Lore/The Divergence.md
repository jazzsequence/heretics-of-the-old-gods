# The Divergence

## Description

The moment at the end of the [[The Calamity|Calamity]] when the Prime Deities and Betrayer Gods alike sealed themselves behind the Divine Gate — a metaphysical barrier preventing gods from manifesting physically on the Material Plane. The Divergence is year zero of the Post-Divergence (P.D.) dating system used throughout Exandria.

The Divine Gate does not prevent divine power from reaching the Material Plane entirely — clerics, paladins, and divine magic still function — but it prevents the gods themselves from walking the world. This is why the Calamity could not repeat: gods can influence events but cannot directly participate in them.

**Relationship to the Exodus:** The gods chose to abandon the Divine Gate's protection at the Exodus, either departing entirely or choosing mortality. Whether this was voluntary, forced by [[Predathos]]'s influence, or something else is the theological fault line underlying every faction in HotOG.
