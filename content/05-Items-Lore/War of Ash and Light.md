# War of Ash and Light

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (835-836 P.D.):** The war between the Krynn Dynasty and the [[Dwendalian Empire]] triggered by the theft of two Luxon beacons — stolen by [[Essek Thelyss]] at the behest of [[Ludinus Da'leth]] and the [[Cerberus Assembly]], who wanted to study dunamantic power. The war consumed Eastern and Western Wynandir for roughly a year before the Mighty Nein brokered a peace.

Resolved partly through [[Essek Thelyss]]'s confession to the Mighty Nein and his eventual cooperation with them and the [[Cobalt Soul]] in exposing the Assembly's role in instigating the conflict. [[Trent Ikithon]] was arrested. Assembly leadership was fractured.

**Significance to HotOG:** This war was both the immediate predecessor of [[Ludinus Da'leth]]'s Ruby Vanguard operation and the event that exhausted both major Wynandir powers — making the region vulnerable during the Apogee Solstice events of 843 P.D. seven years later.
