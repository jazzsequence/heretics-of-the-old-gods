# The Ashari

## Overview

The Ashari are an ancient order of druids and elemental guardians who have maintained vigil over the rifts between the elemental planes and Exandria since before the Founding. Each of the four Ashari tribes is responsible for one rift — air, fire, earth, and water — and their entire civilization is built around that guardianship.

The Ashari were historically aligned with specific Prime Deities — the Wildmother and Dawnfather chief among them — but their actual function predates divine patronage and does not depend on it. The rifts they guard exist regardless of whether gods are present.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The Ashari still exist and still function. Their numbers have dwindled significantly over a millennium without divine backing, new initiates, or the cultural infrastructure that once supported them. They are far fewer than they once were and carry almost no political weight in the current city-state alliance. What they retain is purpose — the rifts still need guarding, and the Ashari are still the only ones doing it. Their spiritual framework has shifted away from deity worship toward pure elemental practice. They invoke the names of gods as historical figures, not active powers.

Their settlements — [[Zephrah]], [[Pyrah]], [[Terrah]], and [[Vesrah]] — are the last intact remnants of their civilization.
