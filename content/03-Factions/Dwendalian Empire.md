
# Dwendalian Empire

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon:** The dominant human empire of Western Wynandir in Wildemount, ruled by King Bertrand Dwendal during Campaign Two. An authoritarian monarchy regulating magic use, religion, and commerce. Founded the [[Cerberus Assembly]] to consolidate arcane power under Crown authority in the 570s PD. Fought the War of Ash and Light against the Krynn Dynasty (835-836 PD).

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Significantly diminished. Under [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s expansion across the Ashkeeper Peaks, the Empire has shrunk to cover only the lands west of the Eisfus River. Current ruler [[Suria Dwendal II]] holds a mostly honorific title — true power runs through the [[Cerberus Assembly]] under [[Yussa Errenis]].

## Key Members

[[Suria Dwendal II]] — nominal ruler.
[[Yussa Errenis]] — de facto power via the Cerberus Assembly.
