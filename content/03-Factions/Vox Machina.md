# Vox Machina

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign One):** The adventuring party that defeated the Chroma Conclave (a group of chromatic dragons), the Whispered One (Vecna, pre-ascension attempt), and other threats to Tal'Dorei and Exandria. Members: Vax'ildan, Vex'ahlia, Grog Strongjaw, Scanlan Shorthalt, Pike Trickfoot, Keyleth (Voice of the Tempest, potentially still alive in 1000 A.E. given her druidic longevity), Percy de Rolo, and Taryon Darrington.

**Relevance to Nuxan:** Primarily historical. Keyleth of the Air Ashari, as Voice of the Tempest, may be the longest-lived former Vox Machina member — druids of her caliber can extend life significantly, and she is a plausible living link to the pre-Exodus world, though this is an open design question.
