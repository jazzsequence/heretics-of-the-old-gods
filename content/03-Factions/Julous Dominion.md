
# Julous Dominion

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon:** A pre-Empire political entity in Western Wynandir, composed of humans and half-elves, conquered by the Dwendalian Crown in the 560s PD. The nobility of the Julous Dominion were assimilated rather than executed — a concession that led directly to the founding of the [[Cerberus Assembly]] in 570 PD, when Dwendalian mages and assimilated Julous nobility resolved their conflict by submitting to Crown authority.

**Status in Nuxan:** Long dissolved, subsumed entirely into Dwendalian identity. Relevant only as historical context for the Cerberus Assembly's founding.
