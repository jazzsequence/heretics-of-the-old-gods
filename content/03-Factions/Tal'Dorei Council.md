# Tal'Dorei Council

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon:** The governing council of Tal'Dorei, established after Vox Machina's era as the continent moved from monarchy toward a more representative system of governance. Inclusive of various political voices across the continent's regions.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Works to fortify borders, gather resources, and rebuild the Emon Arcanum in response to [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s Consecution War sweeping toward [[Tal'Dorei]]. One of the primary organized military resistances to the Krynn expansion in the Rising Action arc.
