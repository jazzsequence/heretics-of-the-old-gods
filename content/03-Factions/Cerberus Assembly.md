
# Cerberus Assembly

## Overview

**Historical background (pre-Exodus canon):** The Cerberus Assembly was a council of extremely powerful archmages in the [[Dwendalian Empire]], centered in [[Rexxentrum]], founded around 570 PD after a conflict between Dwendalian mages and assimilated [[Julous Dominion]] nobility was resolved by submission to the Crown rather than punishment. Composed of eight archmages each overseeing a distinct portfolio (civic influence, diplomacy, domestic protection, etc.), the Assembly grew over centuries into a political and military force some considered capable of rivaling the king himself.

Archmages of the Assembly were major or minor antagonists across all three prior campaigns — [[Delilah Briarwood]], [[Trent Ikithon]], [[Vess DeRogna]], and [[Ludinus Da'leth]] (a founding member and eventual leader) among them. By the Apogee Solstice of 843 PD, most members had abandoned the Empire and disappeared, many aligned with Ludinus Da'leth and the [[Ruby Vanguard]]'s plot.

**This 843 PD disappearance roughly coincides with the dating of the Exodus itself** (the same year Campaign Three's events conclude), making it a natural seam for whatever the Assembly became, or didn't become, in the thousand years since.

**Current status in Nuxan: undeveloped (TBD)** per the source material. Whether any remnant, successor organization, or scattered legacy of the Assembly persists in the post-Exodus world is not yet decided — do not invent this without explicit direction.
