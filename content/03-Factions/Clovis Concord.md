
# Clovis Concord

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon:** A coalition of eight city-states along the [[Menagerie Coast]] bound by a trade compact rather than a unified government. Each city-state maintained its own governance while cooperating on trade regulations, maritime law, and collective defense. The Concord was the primary political structure of the Menagerie Coast throughout [[Critical Role Campaign One|Campaign One]] and [[Critical Role Campaign Two|Campaign Two]].

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Effectively dissolved. The city-states of the former Clovis Concord are the target of [[The Plank King]]'s annexation campaign during the Climax arc — his undead army overruns the harbors and shipyards, depopulating them. The Concord as a governing body no longer exists by the time the campaign opens.
