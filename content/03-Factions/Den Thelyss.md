
# Den Thelyss

## Overview

One of the great Dens (noble houses) of the Krynn Dynasty. Led by Deirta Thelyss (pre-Exodus era), mother of [[Essek Thelyss]]. Dens in the Krynn Dynasty function as extended family-political units that collectively govern the Dynasty's social structure alongside the Bright Queen.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Not specifically established. Den Thelyss continues to exist as an institution within the Krynn Dynasty's governance structure, though [[Essek Thelyss]]'s status 1000 years after the Exodus is an open design question.

## Key Members

[[Essek Thelyss]] — former Shadowhand of the Dynasty.
