
# The Garrotte Line

## Overview

**Origin:** Descended from [[The Clasp]] crime syndicate.

**Belief:** Everything has a price. Secrets are the most valuable.

## Goals & Methods

**Tactics:** A wide, loose criminal organization across Nuxan that buys and sells everything, but especially operates in opposition to [[The Luxon]].
