
# The Mighty Nein

## Overview

The adventuring party at the center of [[Critical Role Campaign Two|Campaign 2]], operating primarily in [[Wildemount]] during the 835-836 P.D. period. Members: [[Caleb Widogast]], [[Beauregard Lionett]], Jester Lavorre, Fjord Stone, Yasha Nydoorin, Veth Brenatto, Caduceus Clay, and Mollymauk Tealeaf (deceased, later Lucien). Kingsley Tealeaf at the end.

Their actions ended the [[War of Ash and Light]], exposed [[Trent Ikithon]]'s crimes, and established the conditions that shaped the [[Cerberus Assembly]]'s post-war diminishment. [[Essek Thelyss]]'s involvement with and eventual redemption through the Mighty Nein is a thread that connects Campaign Two directly to the setting's pre-Exodus political landscape.
