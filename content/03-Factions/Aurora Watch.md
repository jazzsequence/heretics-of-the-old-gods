
# Aurora Watch

## Overview

The military arm of the Krynn Dynasty — the standing army of [[Rosohna]] and the expanded Krynn territory. Commanded historically by the Dusk Captain, a title held by [[Quana Kryn]] in the pre-Exodus period.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Still active as the Krynn Dynasty's military force, significantly expanded as Caelestis's Consecution War pushes into Western Wynandir and eventually toward [[Vasselheim]]. The forces arriving in [[Bassuras]] weeks after [[Auris Vel]]'s coup are Aurora Watch units operating in support of the Luxon mission's political consolidation.

## Key Members

[[Quana Kryn]] — historical Dusk Captain.
[[Soryn Vossira]] — current Overseer of Light, missionary deployment.
