
# Ruby Vanguard

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon ([[Critical Role Campaign Three|Campaign Three]]):** The organization assembled by [[Ludinus Da'leth]] to execute his plan to travel to [[Ruidus]] and free Predathos — the Primordial entity imprisoned within the moon that consumed gods. Composed of true believers in Ludinus's anti-divine ideology alongside recruited agents, mercenaries, and infiltrators placed across major institutions.

The Ruby Vanguard succeeded in freeing Predathos, but failed at the actual goal — destroying the gods. The gods escaped by accepting mortality through the Rites of Catatheosis, becoming invisible to Predathos, which then departed on its own, finding nothing left to consume. Ludinus himself dies on-screen confronting Bells Hells and the Arch Heart at the ruins of Aeor (episode 118, "The Hallowed Cage") — this death is unambiguous in CR canon itself.

**HotOG homebrew addition (confirmed canon for this campaign):** Ludinus did not simply die — the Dark Powers of the Domains of Dread claimed him at the moment of death, and he now exists as the Darklord of Molaesmyr. This is *not* a Critical Role canon event; it's this campaign's own retcon/extension layered onto his confirmed death, tying into the Van Richten's Guide material this project draws from.

**Status in Nuxan:** Dissolved after Ruidus. Whatever remained of the organization scattered. The ideology persists in the [[Whispered Rebellion]] and other anti-Luxon factions, though none claim direct lineage.
