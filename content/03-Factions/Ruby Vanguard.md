
# Ruby Vanguard

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Three):** The organization assembled by [[Ludinus Da'leth]] to execute his plan to travel to [[Ruidus]] and free Predathos — the Primordial entity imprisoned within the moon that consumed gods. Composed of true believers in Ludinus's anti-divine ideology alongside recruited agents, mercenaries, and infiltrators placed across major institutions.

The Ruby Vanguard's operation ultimately failed when Predathos was re-sealed rather than freed. Ludinus himself was taken by the Dark Powers and now serves as Darklord of [[Molaesmyr|Molaesmyr]].

**Status in Nuxan:** Dissolved after Ruidus. Whatever remained of the organization scattered. The ideology persists in the [[Whispered Rebellion]] and other anti-Luxon factions, though none claim direct lineage.
