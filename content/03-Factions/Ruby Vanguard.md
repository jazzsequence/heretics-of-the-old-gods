
# Ruby Vanguard

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon ([[Critical Role Campaign Three|Campaign Three]]):** The organization assembled by [[Ludinus Da'leth]] to execute his plan to travel to [[Ruidus]] and free Predathos — the Primordial entity imprisoned within the moon that consumed gods. Composed of true believers in Ludinus's anti-divine ideology alongside recruited agents, mercenaries, and infiltrators placed across major institutions.

**Status in Nuxan:** Dissolved after Ruidus. Whatever remained of the organization scattered. The ideology persists in the [[Whispered Rebellion]] and other anti-Luxon factions, though none claim direct lineage.
