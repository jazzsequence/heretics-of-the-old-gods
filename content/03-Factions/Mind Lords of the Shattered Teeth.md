# Mind Lords of the Shattered Teeth

## Overview

Three ancient psionicists — [[Thesik]], [[Barani]], and [[Kosveret]] — who transferred their consciousnesses into obsidian orbs millennia ago to preserve their power and their carefully maintained corner of the world at the [[Shattered Teeth]]. Originally from the Dark Sun setting's Mind Lords of the Lost Sea, adapted as a Nuxan-native institution.

They are not a faction in an operational sense — each is an individual in an orb, following their own agendas, constrained by the obligations of keeping the Shattered Teeth civilization alive. Their shared history and proximity does not translate to unified purpose; Thesik's acceleration agenda, Barani's oscillating states, and Kosveret's scattered personalities are all pulling different directions.

## Key Members

[[Thesik]] — first among the Mind Lords, god of death, acceleration ideologue.
[[Barani]] — earth mother, oscillating between warmth and cruelty.
[[Kosveret]] — former god of joy, now a chaos engine with multiple dissociated personalities.
