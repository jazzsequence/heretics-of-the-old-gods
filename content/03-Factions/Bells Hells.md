
# Bells Hells

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Three):** The adventuring party at the center of Campaign Three. Members: Imogen Temult, Laudna, Fearne Calloway, Orym, Ashton Greymoore, Fresh Cut Grass, Chetney Pock o'Pea, and Dorian Storm (intermittent). Operated primarily in [[Marquet]] and later across Exandria during the Ruidus arc.

Their actions directly shaped the conditions that led to the Exodus — the sealing of [[Predathos]], the fate of [[Vecna]], and the 843 PD meeting where the gods debated and ultimately chose departure or mortality.

[[Ashton Greymoore]] has become a myth and folk legend known as the Sanctash, a patron saint of [[Bassuras]]
