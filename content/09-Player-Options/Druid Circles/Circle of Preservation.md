
# Circle of Preservation

*Druid Subclass — Apocalyptic Subclasses UA, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Someone has to work against the collapse instead of accepting it. Preservation druids tend active sanctuaries — literal zones of maintained life in terrain that has given up — and they will defend those zones with the same ferocity as anyone on this list. The work is the opposite of passive.
