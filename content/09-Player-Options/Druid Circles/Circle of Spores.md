
# Circle of Spores

*Druid Subclass — Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica / Tasha's Cauldron of Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Decomposition as the other face of growth. In a world where ecological systems collapsed and what replaced them is stranger and less legible, spore druids occupy the transition between what died and what's growing through it.
