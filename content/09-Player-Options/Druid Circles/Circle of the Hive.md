
# Circle of the Hive

*Druid Subclass — Heliana's Guide to Monster Hunting, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Arthropod symbiotes living in and on the druid's body. Wild Shape becomes the swarm rather than a single creature. In Bassuras, practitioners are identifiable by the quiet buzz that accompanies them and the way other people's skin crawls in their vicinity.
