
# Circle of Cataclysms

*Druid Subclass — Scavenger, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

These druids have accepted that the world is ending — not as defeat, but as fact — and found in that acceptance a specific kind of power. The disasters they channel are real: floods, earthquakes, storms, heat. The desert is not metaphor for them. The desert is the point.
