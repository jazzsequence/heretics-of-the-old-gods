
# Wasteland-Hardened

*General feat (Prerequisite: level 4+) — Campaign homebrew for Heretics of the Old Gods*

The waste took everything soft a long time ago. What's left doesn't burn, doesn't parch, and doesn't quit. You gain the following benefits:

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- **Hardy Frame.** Your Hit Point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your level when you gain this feat, and by 2 each time you gain a level thereafter.
- **Made for the Waste.** You automatically succeed on saving throws against the effects of extreme heat and extreme cold. In addition, you need only half the normal amount of food and water to avoid Exhaustion from starvation and dehydration.
- **Sure-Footed in the Dust.** Difficult terrain made of sand, loose ground, rubble, or debris costs you no extra movement.
