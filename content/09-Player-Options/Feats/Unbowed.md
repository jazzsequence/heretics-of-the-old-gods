
# Unbowed

*General feat (Prerequisite: level 4+) — Campaign homebrew for Heretics of the Old Gods*

You've seen the thing behind the beacon-light and you did not look away, and you did not break. Whatever's left of you after that, nothing lesser is going to move it. You gain the following benefits:

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Wisdom or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- **Steeled Mind.** You have Resistance to psychic damage.
- **Unshakable.** You have Advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Frightened and Charmed conditions.
