
# Ordnance Expert

*General feat (Prerequisite: level 4+) — Campaign homebrew for Heretics of the Old Gods*

You've mastered the delivery and placement of explosives. You gain the following benefits:

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- **Extended Reach.** The range at which you can throw or place explosive devices — including [[Demolitionist]] bombs and thrown alchemical items — doubles.
- **Controlled Blast.** When you detonate an explosive that affects an area, you can choose a number of creatures you can see, up to your proficiency bonus, to be unaffected. They automatically succeed on their saving throw against the effect and take no damage from it.
- **Clear Line.** You ignore half cover and three-quarters cover when determining a creature's position within an explosive's blast area.
