
# Deathless

*General feat (Prerequisite: level 4+) — Campaign homebrew for Heretics of the Old Gods*

Resurrection in the post-Exodus world of Nuxan is never a guarantee and no one is owed anything. In a world where death is an inevitability in certain occupations, you rely on your animal refusal to succumb to its cold embrace. You gain the following benefits:

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- **Relentless.** When you are reduced to 0 Hit Points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 Hit Point instead. Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
