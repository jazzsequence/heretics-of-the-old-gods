
# Alchemist

*Artificer Subclass — Forge of the Artificer, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The Hellcatch's folk medicine tradition runs on what can be harvested, brewed, and administered without institutional approval. An Alchemist Wrench in Bassuras is the person people go to when they can't afford or can't risk the [[Luxon]] mission house.
