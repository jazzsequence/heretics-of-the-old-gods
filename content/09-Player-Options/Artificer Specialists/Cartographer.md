
# Cartographer

*Artificer Subclass — Forge of the Artificer, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Less mapmaker, more reconnaissance specialist. Their tools mark targets, open short-range portals, and let allies act on information they can't see directly — useful for a dig crew where half the danger is not knowing what's two chambers ahead, and the other half is getting someone out fast when it goes wrong.
