
# The Horned King Patron

*Warlock Subclass — The Crooked Moon, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Wild, fey-adjacent, the beast that stalks the deepest woods. The Feywild is not easily reached from Nuxan — it's sealed off, diminished, being slowly drained by the [[Luxon]]'s light-beacon infrastructure — which means a Horned King patron requires a backstory explaining how contact was established. The pact is sealed in blood. The patron has a coven-forming capstone. Neither of these things is metaphorical.
