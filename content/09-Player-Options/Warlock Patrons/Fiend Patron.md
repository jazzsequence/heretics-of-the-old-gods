
# Fiend Patron

*Warlock Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The infernal hierarchy predates the [[Exodus]] by millennia and survived it intact. A fiend's pact is possible in Nuxan, but requires backstory explaining how contact was made — the kind of entity that grants Fiend warlock power doesn't come knocking without reason.
