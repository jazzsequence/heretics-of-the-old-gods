
# The Great Fool Patron

*Warlock Subclass — The Crooked Moon, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

A powerful otherworldly being of mockery and misrule whose designs are genuinely incomprehensible and whose sense of humor does not operate on human logic. Great Fool warlocks are granted power through an arrangement the patron finds funny. Whether what it finds funny is the patron's goals or the warlock's situation is not something the Great Fool chooses to clarify.
