# GUNNER

Firearms have existed in [[Nuxan]] for a thousand years, since Percival de Rolo built the first one in [[Whitestone]]. In that time they've spread, degraded, been improvised, rebuilt, and jury-rigged past any resemblance to Percy's original precision engineering. What's available in [[Bassuras]] ranges from masterwork to actively dangerous to the person holding it. A Gunner isn't a Fighter who happens to carry a gun — this is a class built entirely around what a firearm makes possible.

## Available Subclasses

- **Bulletsmith** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — The ammunition is the weapon. A Bulletsmith's rounds are modified, crafted, and loaded for specific purposes, and the gap between what they can do and what a standard round does is the entire point of the subclass.
- **Chronotrigger** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — Time-displacement integrated into gunfighting technique. The Chronotrigger's relationship to dunamantic tradition is either studied or stumbled into, and in Nuxan the line between those two origins has specific political weight.
- **Deadeye** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — Precision marksmanship stripped to its fundamentals. In the Hellcatch, where dust storms reduce visibility and wind patterns are disrupted by the snarl's heat bleed, a Deadeye's ability to put a round where they intend it at range is a rarer skill than it sounds.
- **Slugger** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — Heavy weapons at close range, maximum damage output, minimal concern for elegance. The Blood Pits have produced this. So have raider bands and the less formal enforcement operations of the [[Court of Dust]].
- **Spellslinger** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — Something happens when they pull the trigger. They didn't study for this — it's in them, and the gun is the only delivery mechanism they've found that doesn't immediately go sideways. In a world where unauthorized arcane practice is illegal, someone whose magic runs through a weapon occupies an unusual gray zone that neither the [[Cerberus Assembly]] nor the [[Luxon]] church has a clean framework for.
- **Dragoon** *(Zaman's Guide to the End of Time)* — Mounted gunfighting, adapted for Bassuras's crawler and desert mount culture. A Dragoon on the Flats is a specific tactical problem for raider bands, who have learned that the mounted figure firing from a moving platform is always a priority target and almost never an easy one.
