
# Path of the Storm Herald

*Barbarian Subclass — Xanathar's Guide to Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The Hellcatch storms are [[Ur'Shakthu]]'s voice. A Storm Herald who chose the desert aura is channeling something the Hellcatch has been living with for a thousand years. Whether that connection is theological or incidental, the storm responds.
