
# Path of the Fanatic

*Barbarian Subclass — PHB 2024 (Zealot, reskinned), adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Not divine frenzy. The Exodus took the gods. It did not take belief, and in Nuxan, belief without gods to constrain it has curdled into something harder and more dangerous. A Fanatic fights for an idea they can't survive without holding.
