
# Path of the Ancestral Guardian

*Barbarian Subclass — Xanathar's Guide to Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The dead accumulate in Nuxan's ungoverned [[Shadowfell]]. An Ancestral Guardian has established a relationship with specific dead — their dead — who answer when called. Whether what answers is actually their ancestors is a question they've learned not to ask.
