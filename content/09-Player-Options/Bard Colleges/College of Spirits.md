
# College of Spirits

*Bard Subclass — Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Storytelling that conjures and channels the dead — not metaphorically. In a world with a thousand years of ungoverned souls accumulating in the [[Shadowfell]], what answers when a Spirits Bard calls may not be what they expected, or who.
