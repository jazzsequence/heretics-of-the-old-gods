
# Grave Domain

*Cleric Subclass — Xanathar's Guide to Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Available only to characters with [[Echoes of the Raven]] or [[House Corvane]] faction ties. The Echoes believe natural death is dignity and have no idea what the [[Shadowfell]] has become. House Corvane knows exactly what it's become and considers it an opportunity. Both can access this domain. Their purposes are not the same.
