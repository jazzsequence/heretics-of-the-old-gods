
# Harvest Domain

*Cleric Subclass — The Crooked Moon, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Agricultural death cycle as power source, rooted in rural folk practice that predates [[Bassuras]] by centuries. Harvest clerics tend communities that grow what they can in hostile conditions and sanctify the ground so it produces at all. There's nothing gentle about what they do — you can't bless a field without first understanding what the field costs.
