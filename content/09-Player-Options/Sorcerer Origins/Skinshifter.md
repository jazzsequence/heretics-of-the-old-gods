
# Skinshifter

*Sorcerer Subclass — Heliana's Guide to Monster Hunting, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The spells change the body. Sometimes intentionally. More often as a byproduct — a mutation that surfaces when a spell fires, a physical transformation triggered by magical expenditure. The adaptations are unpredictable by design. Some of them are advantages. Some of them are not.
