
# Wild Magic Sorcery

*Sorcerer Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The snarl beneath the Hellcatch bleeds wild magic upward through the rock on no predictable schedule. A Wild Magic sorcerer from [[Bassuras]] isn't surging randomly — they're resonating with a specific, destabilized source, and the surges they produce carry its particular signature.
