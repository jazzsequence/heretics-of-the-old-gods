
# Shadow Magic

*Sorcerer Subclass — Xanathar's Guide to Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

[[Shadowfell]] residue in the bloodline, which in Nuxan means a thousand years of ungoverned accumulation as the source material. A Shadow Magic sorcerer drawing on that is drawing on something that has been getting louder, more complex, and more dangerous for ten centuries.
