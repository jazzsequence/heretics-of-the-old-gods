
# Aberrant Mind

*Sorcerer Subclass — Tasha's Cauldron of Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Something from outside the normal order of reality made itself at home in the bloodline. In Nuxan, where the Far Realm influence of the Eldrazi war left scars in the astral fabric that haven't fully closed, Aberrant Mind sorcerers may be drawing on something with a specific and traceable source.
