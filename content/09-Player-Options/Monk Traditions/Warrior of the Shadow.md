
# Warrior of the Shadow

*Monk Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Shadow Step in a city of unmarked [[Court of Dust]] spies and [[Garrotte Line]] operatives. A Shadow Warrior's relationship to the [[Shadowfell]]'s bleed — which is increasingly visible in Bassuras — is complicated by the fact that what they're drawing on is getting louder.
