
# Way of the Sun Soul

*Monk Subclass — Xanathar's Guide to Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Radiant energy channeled through martial discipline. The light it generates is not divine — the gods are gone. What it actually is remains an open question that Sun Soul monks in Nuxan have learned to stop asking, because the answers available are unsatisfying and the power works regardless.
