
# Way of the Pestilent Haze

*Monk Subclass — The Crooked Moon, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

A discipline that weaponizes disease through martial practice, and whose origin nobody in Bassuras can fully account for. Pestilent Haze practitioners aren't common, aren't organized, and aren't welcome in most of the city's faction structures. They tend to operate alone, which suits them.
