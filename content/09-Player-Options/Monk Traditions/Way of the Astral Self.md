
# Way of the Astral Self

*Monk Subclass — Tasha's Cauldron of Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Psionic self-projection, the astral body manifesting as a combat tool. In Nuxan, psionic ability predates and survives the divine collapse intact — it's one of the few power systems that never required the gods to function, which gives Astral Self monks a quiet authority in a world that's lost everything else.
