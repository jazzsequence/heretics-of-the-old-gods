
# Way of Mercy

*Monk Subclass — Tasha's Cauldron of Everything, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Healing and lethal capability fused in the same discipline. In Bassuras, where the mission house and the back-alley surgery are both real and both necessary, a Mercy monk occupies the specific niche of someone who can walk into a bad situation and determine, with accuracy, which problem needs solving first.
