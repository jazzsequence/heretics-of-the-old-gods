
# Way of the Cobalt Soul

*Monk Subclass — Tal'Dorei Campaign Setting Reborn, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The Cobalt Soul became the [[Cobalt Veil]], and the Veil operates as an underground information network rather than an open academic institution. A Cobalt Soul monk in this campaign has a built-in connection to a faction that's actively archiving what the [[Luxon]] wants erased.
