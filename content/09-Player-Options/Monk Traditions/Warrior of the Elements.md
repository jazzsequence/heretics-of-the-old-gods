
# Warrior of the Elements

*Monk Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Elemental attunement without divine mediation, drawing directly on the same forces the hedge god clerics propitiate. In the Hellcatch, that means a specific relationship with desert heat, underground water, the snarl's electrical bleed, and wind that carries things it shouldn't.
