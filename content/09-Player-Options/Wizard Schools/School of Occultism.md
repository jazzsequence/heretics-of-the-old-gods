
# School of Occultism

*Wizard Subclass — The Crooked Moon, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Forbidden knowledge from beyond the universe, accessed through a risk-escalating intrusion mechanic — the further you push, the more gets through from the other side. A hedge-witch tradition by nature rather than an institutional one. An Occultist in Nuxan is pulling at the same Far Realm-adjacent fabric that the Eldrazi war left scarred, and the effects that result from going too far are neither metaphorical nor controlled.
