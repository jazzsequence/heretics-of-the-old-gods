
# School of the Unseen

*Wizard Subclass — Heretics of the Old Gods homebrew, merged from the Diviner and Illusionist (PHB 2024)*

## Overview

Knowing the hidden truth and hiding the truth are one skillset. As the academies contracted, the two schools that dealt in perception — Divination and Illusion — folded into a single discipline built for intelligence work. An Unseen wizard sees what others can't and shows others what isn't there. The [[Court of Dust]] recruits them as augurs; the [[Garrotte Line]] values them for reasons it doesn't discuss. In practice they are the same profession: the person in the room who knows more than they should, and can't be trusted about any of it.

## Features

### Foresight and Misdirection (Level 3)

**Portent.** When you finish a long rest, roll two d20s and record the numbers. You can replace any D20 Test made by you or a creature you can see with one of these foretelling rolls — before the roll is made, and only once per turn. Each roll is used once; unused rolls are lost at your next long rest.

**Improved Minor Illusion.** You know the *Minor Illusion* cantrip. When you cast it, you can create both a sound and an image with a single casting. If you already knew *Minor Illusion*, you instead learn one additional wizard cantrip of your choice.

### Expert Divination (Level 6)

When you cast a divination spell using a level 2+ spell slot, you regain one expended spell slot. The regained slot must be of a lower level than the one you expended, and can't be higher than level 5.

### The Third Eye (Level 10)

As a Bonus Action, choose one benefit, lasting until you start a short or long rest. You can't use this again until you finish a rest:

- **Darkvision** out to 120 feet.
- **Greater Comprehension:** you can read any language.
- **See Invisibility:** you can cast *See Invisibility* without expending a spell slot.

### Illusory Reality (Level 14)

When you cast an illusion spell using a spell slot, you can choose one inanimate, nonmagical object that is part of the illusion and make it real, as a Bonus Action while the spell is ongoing. The object stays real for 1 minute and can't deal damage or impose conditions.

## Note on the Lost Curriculum

Two capstone techniques of the old schools were cut in the merge and survive only as [[Lost Art Feats|Lost Art]] fragments: **Greater Portent** (three foretelling dice) and **Illusory Self** (a reactive illusory duplicate). An Unseen wizard who wants either must reconstruct it.
