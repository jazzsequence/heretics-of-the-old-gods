
# School of Necromancy

*Wizard Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

In a world with an ungoverned [[Shadowfell]] full of accumulated dead, Necromancy isn't a fringe specialization — it's a school with constant, pressing relevance. A Necromancer is either a practical tool or a catastrophic liability depending on what they're reaching toward and why. The [[Echoes of the Raven]] and the [[Cult of Death]] both have opinions about Necromancers, and those opinions are not compatible with each other.
