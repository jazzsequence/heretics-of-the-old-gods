
# School of Bladesinging

*Wizard Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Elven arcane martial tradition, repurposed in Nuxan as the foot soldiers of institutional power. A Bladesinger is typically [[Cerberus Assembly]] muscle or a [[Kryn Dynasty]] asset operating under Assembly cover — magic integrated into combat discipline closely enough to read as physical training from a distance, which is exactly the cover the institutions sponsoring it want.
