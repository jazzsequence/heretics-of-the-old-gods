
# School of Evocation

*Wizard Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Raw destructive output, carefully targeted. Evocation is one of the schools where shared knowledge and common practice survived the collapse most intact — an Evoker in Nuxan is either employed as muscle by someone with enough resources to protect them from the Assembly's attention, or they're very good at not being found.
