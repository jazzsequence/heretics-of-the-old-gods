
# School of Blood Magic

*Wizard Subclass — Tal'Dorei Campaign Setting Reborn, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Blood as fuel and sensor, the arcane tradition of the [[Claret Orders]]' wizard-adjacent practitioners. Blood is always available, which makes this one of the more resilient traditions to survive the collapse of formal institutions. This knowledge isn't widely taught, and coming into it means coming into contact with the Orders.
