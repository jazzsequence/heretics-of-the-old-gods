
# Spellslinger

*Gunner Subclass — Zaman's Guide to the End of Time, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Something happens when they pull the trigger. They didn't study for this — it's in them, and the gun is the only delivery mechanism they've found that doesn't immediately go sideways. In a world where unauthorized arcane practice is illegal, someone whose magic runs through a weapon occupies an unusual gray zone that neither the [[Cerberus Assembly]] nor the [[Luxon]] church has a clean framework for.
