
# Dragoon

*Gunner Subclass — Zaman's Guide to the End of Time, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Mounted gunfighting, adapted for Bassuras's crawler and desert mount culture. A Dragoon on the Flats is a specific tactical problem for raider bands, who have learned that the mounted figure firing from a moving platform is always a priority target and almost never an easy one.
