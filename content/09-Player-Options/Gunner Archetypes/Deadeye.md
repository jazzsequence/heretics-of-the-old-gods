
# Deadeye

*Gunner Subclass — Zaman's Guide to the End of Time, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Precision marksmanship stripped to its fundamentals. In the Hellcatch, where dust storms reduce visibility and wind patterns are disrupted by the snarl's heat bleed, a Deadeye's ability to put a round where they intend it at range is a rarer skill than it sounds.
