
# Guardian

*Ranger Subclass — Hexbound, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

This tradition works with ancestor spirits rather than nature spirits — the dead who are invested in specific places and specific people, called for particular purposes. In Nuxan's ungoverned [[Shadowfell]], establishing those relationships requires care, because what you're reaching toward has had a thousand years to become something other than what it started as.
