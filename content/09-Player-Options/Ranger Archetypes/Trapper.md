
# Trapper

*Ranger Subclass — Heliana's Guide to Monster Hunting, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Prepared battlefield as primary combat philosophy. Physical and magical traps, thieves' tools proficiency, the discipline of controlling how and where a fight happens rather than just surviving it. In a city where the geography is layered with ruins and narrow underground passages, a Trapper's ability to shape the engagement is significant.
