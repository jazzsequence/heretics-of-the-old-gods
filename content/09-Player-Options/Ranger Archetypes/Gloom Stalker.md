
# Gloom Stalker

*Ranger Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The [[Shadowfell]] bleeds into [[Bassuras]] in the dark. The ungoverned dead are louder at night, and the boundary between planes is thinner than it used to be. A Gloom Stalker in [[Nuxan]] is tracking things that move in that bleed — which is increasingly most of the dangerous things.
