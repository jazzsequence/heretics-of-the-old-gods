
# Psi Warrior

*Fighter Subclass — PHB 2024, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Psionic ability in Nuxan is old, pre-divine, and doesn't require magical authorization. A Psi Warrior operates entirely outside the [[Luxon]]'s framework for what power is supposed to look like, which creates a specific kind of institutional anxiety in people who encounter them.
