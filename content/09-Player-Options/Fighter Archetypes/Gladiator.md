
# Gladiator

*Fighter Subclass — Apocalyptic Subclasses UA, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

The Blood Pits exist. The racing syndicates have informal bouts. The [[Court of Dust]] uses spectacle as population management. Gladiator discipline is theatrical, Charisma-driven combat — performance and survival fused into a single skill set, because in the arena they were never separate.
