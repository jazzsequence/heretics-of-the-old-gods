
# Echo Knight

*Fighter Subclass — Explorer's Guide to Wildemount, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Dunamantic tradition originating with the [[Kryn Dynasty]]. An Echo Knight pulls a version of themselves from a timeline that didn't happen and weaponizes the gap between what was and what is. In Nuxan, a PC with this training either has Kryn Dynasty ties or acquired the knowledge from sources that will make those ties complicated.
