
# Falconer

*Rogue Subclass — Zaman's Guide to the End of Time, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

A bird of prey as steadfast partner rather than tool. Desert guides and Flats scouts have always worked with trained raptors for reconnaissance; a Falconer takes that practical relationship further, fighting in genuine coordination with a hunting bird that's seen as much of the Hellcatch as they have.
