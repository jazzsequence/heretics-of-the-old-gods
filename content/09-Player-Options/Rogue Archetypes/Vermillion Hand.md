
# Vermillion Hand

*Rogue Subclass — Scavenger, adapted for Heretics of the Old Gods*

## Overview

Shadow projection and remote operation. A Vermillion Hand can be somewhere they aren't, operate through a silhouette that contains their abilities, and act from a different location while their body stays somewhere safer. In a city of unmarked [[Court of Dust]] agents and [[Garrotte Line]] operatives, that is not a small advantage.
