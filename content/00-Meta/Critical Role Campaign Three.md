
# Critical Role Campaign Three

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**DM:** Matthew Mercer. **Party:** Bells Hells — Imogen Temult, Laudna, Fearne Calloway, Orym, Ashton Greymoore, Chetney Pock O'Pea, Fresh Cut Grass (FCG), later joined by Braius Doomseed and Deanna Leimert's circle.

Aired 2021–2025. Set primarily on Marquet. Central plot: the mystery of Ruidus, the Ruidusborn, Ludinus Da'leth's Ruby Vanguard, and the Predathos threat. Concludes at the **Apogee Solstice** and the Rites of Catatheosis, in which the gods choose to bind themselves to mortality rather than remain vulnerable to Predathos, shattering the Divine Gate.

**Status for this project:** This is the Exodus. Campaign Three's conclusion (843 PD) is year 0 A.E. — the founding trauma of Nuxan and the fixed point HotOG is set 1000 years after.
