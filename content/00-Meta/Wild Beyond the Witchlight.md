
# The Wild Beyond the Witchlight

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**Publisher:** Wizards of the Coast, 2021. A levels 1–8 adventure centered on the Witchlight Carnival, a traveling fair that serves as a gateway to Prismeer, a Feywild domain ruled (and warped) by the Hourglass Coven of hags. Tonally whimsical/fairy-tale, with player choice heavily shaping the outcome of Prismeer's ruling archfey.

**Status for this project:** Whatever happened during this arc in Exandria Revisited (including which archfey ended up ruling Prismeer, and Prismeer's fate) is canon/ancient history for HotOG. Directly relevant to the sealed Feywild premise — Prismeer's post-Exodus status, if referenced, needs to be reconciled with the general Feywild seal rather than treated as an exception by default.
