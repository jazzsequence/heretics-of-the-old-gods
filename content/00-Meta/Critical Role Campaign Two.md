
# Critical Role Campaign Two

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**DM:** Matthew Mercer. **Party:** The Mighty Nein — Caleb Widogast, Beauregard Lionett, Jester Lavorre, Fjord Stone, Yasha Nydoorin, Veth Brenatto (Nott), Caduceus Clay, Mollymauk Tealeaf (later Lucien), Kingsley Tealeaf.

Aired 2018–2021. Set primarily in Wildemount — the Dwendalian Empire and Xhorhas (Kryn Dynasty territory). Covers the War of Ash and Light, the fall of Trent Ikithon's conditioning program, and the Mighty Nein's dealings with the Cerberus Assembly and the Kryn Dynasty.

**Status for this project:** All events are historical canon, roughly 1000 years prior to Heretics of the Old Gods.
