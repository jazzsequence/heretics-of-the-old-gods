
# Planescape

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**Publisher:** Wizards of the Coast, most recently *Planescape: Adventures in the Multiverse* (2023 boxed set), building on the original 1994 TSR setting. Centers on Sigil, the City of Doors — a torus-shaped city built around the spire at the center of the Outlands, containing portals to every plane of existence, ruled by the enigmatic and near-omnipotent Lady of Pain. The Outlands itself is the neutral plane bridging the Outer Planes, ringed by gate-towns each aligned toward a different plane's philosophy.

Only the specific elements Chris incorporated (the city of Sigil and the Outlands) are confirmed part of this project's scope — not necessarily the full classic faction system or the wider Great Wheel cosmology unless separately established.

**Status for this project:** Whatever role Sigil/the Outlands played in Exandria Revisited (likely connected to the Vecna: Eve of Ruin arc, since that adventure routes through Sigil) is canon/ancient history for HotOG.
