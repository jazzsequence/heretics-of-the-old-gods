
# Critical Role Campaign Four

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**DM:** Brennan Lee Mulligan (Matthew Mercer plays a PC for the first time). Premiered October 2, 2025. **Not set in Exandria** — takes place in a new world, Aramán, following the mortal overthrow of that world's gods (the Shapers). West Marches format, rotating cast across three tables (Soldiers, Seekers, Schemers).

**Status for this project:** Not lore-canon for Nuxan or Exandria — different world entirely. Referenced in this vault **only** as a source of mechanical homebrew (e.g. the Desperate Measures death-save mechanic in `11-DM-Notes/`). Do not treat any Aramán setting details, NPCs, or plot as applicable to Nuxan.
