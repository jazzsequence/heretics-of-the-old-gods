
# Ravnica

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**Publisher:** Wizards of the Coast, *Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica* (2018), adapting the Magic: the Gathering plane of Ravnica — a world entirely covered by a single city-plane, governed by ten guilds instead of conventional nation-states. Relevant guilds for this project: **House Dimir** (secrecy, misinformation, and assassination, operating almost entirely through fronts and cutouts) and **the Izzet League** (chaotic magical/mad-science experimentation, elemental combustion magic).

**Status for this project:** Whatever role Ravnica (or elements/agents of Dimir and Izzet specifically) played in Exandria Revisited is canon/ancient history for HotOG — likely incorporated as factional influences or displaced agents rather than the plane itself being folded into Exandria's cosmology, similar to Heckna and Dark Sun.
