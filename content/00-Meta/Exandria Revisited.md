
# Exandria Revisited

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not a Critical Role property — this is Chris's own years-long home campaign, set in Exandria, run prior to Heretics of the Old Gods. Managed in Notion, not this vault.

Incorporated material from *Heckna* (Hit Point Press), *The Wild Beyond the Witchlight*, *Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft*, and culminated in *Vecna: Eve of Ruin* (all Wizards of the Coast/Darrington Press), plus inclusions from Planescape (Sigil and the Outlands), Dark Sun (Mind Lords of the Lost Sea), Ravnica (including the Dimir and Izzet), and Eldrazi creatures from Magic: the Gathering.

**Status for this project:** HotOG is a direct sequel. Events, character fates, and world-state changes from this campaign are canon and count as further ancient history, same as the three CR campaigns — folded into the same 1000-years-later timeline.

Worth building those out the same way once specific plot threads from Exandria Revisited start getting referenced in HotOG notes.
