Incorporated material from [[Heckna]] (Hit Point Press), [[Wild Beyond the Witchlight|The Wild Beyond the Witchlight]], [[Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft]], and culminated in [[Vecna Eve of Ruin|Vecna: Eve of Ruin]] (all Wizards of the Coast/Darrington Press), plus inclusions from [[Planescape]] (Sigil and the Outlands), [[Dark Sun]] (Mind Lords of the Last Sea), [[Ravnica]] (including the Dimir and Izzet), and [[Eldrazi]] from Magic: the Gathering.

**Status for this project:** HotOG is a direct sequel. Events, character fates, and world-state changes from this campaign are canon and count as further ancient history, same as the three CR campaigns — folded into the same 1000-years-later timeline.
