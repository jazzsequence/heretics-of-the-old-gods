
# Vecna: Eve of Ruin

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**Publisher:** Wizards of the Coast, 2024 — D&D's 50th anniversary adventure, levels 11–20. Vecna, having stolen the multiverse-spanning power of the Rod of Seven Parts, attempts to erase and remake all of existence. The adventure is a multiversal crawl through several D&D settings (Ravenloft's Barovia, Spelljammer, Dragonlance, Forgotten Realms, Eberron, and Sigil/the Outer Planes via [[Planescape]]), culminating in a final confrontation with Vecna.

**Status for this project:** This was the culminating adventure of Exandria Revisited, meaning Chris's own campaign's Vecna is explicitly the multiverse-threat version from this book, not (only) the Campaign One Vecna. How these two Vecna threads reconcile — whether they're the same entity at different points, or Exandria Revisited's ending folds the CR-canon Vecna (see [[Vecna]]) into this multiversal one — is a worldbuilding question to resolve before Vecna's post-Exodus status in Nuxan gets locked down further.
