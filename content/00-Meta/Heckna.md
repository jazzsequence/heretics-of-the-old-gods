
# Heckna!

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity.

**Publisher:** Hit Point Press (third-party, Kickstarter-funded, not Wizards of the Coast). A standalone 5e dark carnival horror campaign setting and adventure for levels 1–10, centered on a cosmic carnival called the Revelia, run by the entity Heckna. Corruption and forced "revelry" turn victims into thralls.

Heckna! is not native to Exandria — it's an independent third-party setting. Its incorporation into Exandria Revisited was an adaptation: the Revelia was inserted into (or reachable from) Exandria as an extraplanar location, not treated as originally part of the setting.

**Status for this project:** Whatever happened during the Revelia arc in Exandria Revisited is canon/ancient history for HotOG. If specific plot beats from that arc get referenced in vault notes, they need their own entries the same way CR NPCs do — not yet built out.

Reference: [hitpointpress.com](https://hitpointpress.com/collections/heckna)
