
# Critical Role Campaign One

Real-world source disambiguation note. Not an in-fiction entity — links here so "Campaign One" in body text resolves to an unambiguous reference rather than colliding with Chris's own campaigns (see [[Exandria Revisited]], or HotOG itself).

**DM:** Matthew Mercer. **Party:** Vox Machina — Vax'ildan, Vex'ahlia, Percival de Rolo, Keyleth, Grog Strongjaw, Scanlan Shorthalt, Pike Trickfoot, Tiberius Stormwind (early).

Aired 2015–2017 (originally a private home game from 2013). Set primarily in Tal'Dorei. Covers the Briarwood occupation of Whitestone, the Chroma Conclave, and Vecna's ascension attempt and banishment behind the Divine Gate.

**Status for this project:** All events are historical canon, roughly 1000 years prior to Heretics of the Old Gods.
