- **Exandria Unlimited (Prime)** — DM Aabria Iyengar, 2022. Introduces the Crown Keepers and Marquet-region threads that feed into Campaign Three.
- **Exandria Unlimited: Calamity** — DM Brennan Lee Mulligan, 2023. Set during the Calamity, the war between the Prime Deities and Betrayer Gods.
- **Critical Role: Downfall** — DM Brennan Lee Mulligan, 2024. Three-episode arc set ~100 years into the Calamity, the fall of Aeor.
- **Exandria Unlimited: Divergence** — DM Brennan Lee Mulligan, 2024. Follows survivors imprisoned during the Calamity.
