
# Leylas Kryn

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Empress of the Kryn Dynasty, first Umavi, discoverer of the first Luxon beacon, ruler for over six hundred years with over 1,200 years of life accumulated through consecution. The dynasty was built around her journey. She was deeply revered as a mighty warrior, an empathic leader, and the elder Umavi who guided her people in the light of the Luxon.

Partner through multiple lifetimes with [[Quana Kryn]]. In the late 830s PD, foresaw a child conceived through the Luxon. Caelestis was born and raised while Leylas prolonged her campaign of consecration across Xhorhas, eventually returning to find Caelestis had developed monstrous abilities linked to her true parentage. Leylas ordered Caelestis executed beyond beacon range, though Quana attempted to intervene. Her behavior grew erratic in her final lifetimes, worrying her inner circle.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Dead. The consecution of her soul unable to bear the numerous lifetimes over the course of 1000 years after the Exodus. Her passing, alongside Quana's, transferred the Bright Queen mantle to Caelestis.

## Personality

Driven, empathic, revered. Described as the most ancient non-divine figure in Exandrian history by the end of her life. Her behavior grew increasingly erratic as the weight of lifetimes and the Caelestis situation accumulated.
