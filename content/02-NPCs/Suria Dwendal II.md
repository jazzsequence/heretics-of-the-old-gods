# Suria Dwendal II

## Description

Current ruler of the Dwendalian Empire 1000 years after the Exodus. An elderly woman whose title is mostly honorific — the true power of the empire comes through the [[Cerberus Assembly]] led by [[Yussa Errenis]]. The once great Dwendalian Empire has shrunk under [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s expansion to only cover the lands west of the Eisfus River.

**Note:** Suria Dwendal II is a Nuxan-original figure with no pre-Exodus canon. The name follows the Dwendalian royal naming pattern as a distant descendant. No further detail is established in the source material.
