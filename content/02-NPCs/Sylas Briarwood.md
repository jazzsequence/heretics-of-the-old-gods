
# Sylas Briarwood

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign One):** Husband of [[Delilah Briarwood]]. A vampire, his undead state the direct result of the bargain Delilah struck with [[Vecna]] — she made the pact to save his life, and Vecna's price was his vampirism. Served as Lord of [[Whitestone]] alongside Delilah during the Briarwood arc. Killed by Vox Machina during the Whitestone liberation.

Historical only — confirmed dead pre-Exodus.
