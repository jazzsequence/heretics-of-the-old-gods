
# Sylas Briarwood

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon ([[Critical Role Campaign One|Campaign One]]):** Husband of [[Delilah Briarwood]]. A vampire, his undead state the direct result of the bargain Delilah struck with [[Vecna]] — she made the pact to save his life, and Vecna's price was his vampirism. Served as Lord of [[Whitestone]] alongside Delilah during the Briarwood occupation.

Killed once by Vox Machina during the Whitestone liberation (his mist form destroyed by Vex'ahlia's radiant power). He resurfaced later, alongside a Vecna-resurrected Delilah, at the final battle against Vecna at Thar Amphala — there he escaped in vampiric mist form when Delilah took her final, permanent killing blow. Vox Machina searched for him afterward in the woods near Vasselheim but couldn't relocate him.

**Status:** Not confirmed dead. His final fate is explicitly unresolved in canon.
