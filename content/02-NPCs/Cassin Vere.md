# Cassin Vere

## Description

Human, middle-aged, professional to the point of affectlessness. Runs legitimate contracts as cover for Court-directed work out of a modest office near the Ends. Known across [[Bassuras]] for connecting labor to work without obvious affiliation. Not malicious toward the PCs — they're a transaction.

Posts the Ninth Expedition job through normal channels with legitimate Court authorization stamps. The authorization traces directly to the [[The Survey Foreman|Survey Foreman]]'s office — Cassin is a Court asset routing contracts the Court needs filled by people with no official connection to the organization.

Disappears when the Court consolidates its underground operations. Office closed overnight, no explanation, no forwarding address. Where they went and what they took with them is an open question.

## Personality

Professional detachment as both shield and mode of operation. Accepts contract outcomes without apparent reaction.
