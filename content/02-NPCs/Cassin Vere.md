
# Cassin Vere

## Description

Human, middle-aged, professional to the point of affectlessness. Runs legitimate contracts as cover for Court-directed work out of a modest office near the Ends. Known across [[Bassuras]] for connecting labor to work without obvious affiliation. Not malicious toward the PCs — they're a transaction.

Posts the Ninth Expedition job through normal channels with legitimate Court authorization stamps. The authorization traces directly to the [[The Survey Foreman|Survey Foreman]]'s office.

## Personality

Professional detachment as both shield and mode of operation. Accepts contract outcomes without apparent reaction.
