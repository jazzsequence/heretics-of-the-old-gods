# The Survey Foreman

## Description

Court of Dust official overseeing underground survey operations in [[Bassuras]]. Known only by title — no proper name in use. Bureaucratic, methodical, increasingly alarmed by what his teams encounter below. Not malicious in a personal sense; institutionally incapable of handling what's actually happening. Will escalate to force as a default response because force is the only tool he has.

Arrives at the [[Hook B - The Broken Chain|cave-in site]] with a clipboard before the dust has settled. Begins classifying the incident as operator error before he's finished his first pass. Tells [[Maren Thol]], politely and with complete finality, that the union's safety concerns have been noted and will be reviewed through appropriate channels.

The Court authorization stamps on [[Cassin Vere]]'s contract in [[Hook C - The Ninth Expedition]] trace directly to his office. His name appears on the pre-arrival correspondence with [[Auris Vel]]'s Luxon ecclesiastical seal, linking the Court deal to the Luxon operation. Named in Auris's overheard conversation in [[Hook D - The Congregation]] as someone whose continued presence is a complication to be managed.

Recurs across all four Exposition hooks wherever Court underground operations intersect with the surface world.

## Personality

Bureaucratically certain rather than overtly malicious. Present and clipboard in hand at every stage of the crisis.
