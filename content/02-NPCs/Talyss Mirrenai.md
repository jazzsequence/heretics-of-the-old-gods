# Talyss Mirrenai

## Description

A cadet at the Krynn military academy at the same time as [[Soryn Vossira]]. Shared Soryn's intellect and out-of-the-box thinking. The two fell in love. When their relationship was discovered by their superiors, Talyss was sent off to the Dwendalian Empire and was never heard from again. Her absence is the central loss that shaped Soryn's emotional life — their desperate longing for connection and their terror of being left alone both trace back to this.

## Personality

Described through Soryn's memory only — shared intellect, shared unconventional thinking.
