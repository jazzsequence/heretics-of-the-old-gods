
# Predathos

## Description

A Primordial entity predating the gods of Exandria — one of the first beings to exist in this reality, described as an "eater of gods." The gods feared and imprisoned it within the moon [[Ruidus]] before the Divergence, constructing the moon as a cage rather than a naturally-formed body.

[[Ludinus Da'leth]]'s [[Ruby Vanguard]] sought to travel to [[Ruidus]] and free Predathos at the Apogee Solstice of 843 PD, using it as a weapon to destroy the gods. This ultimately failed — Predathos was re-sealed through the collective effort of Bells Hells and the gods themselves, in what amounted to a temporary alliance against annihilation. The structural damage to Ruidus during this event is significant; the moon is now the shattered [[The Crimson Shadow|Crimson Shadow]] fragments orbiting Nuxan.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Re-sealed. Whether the re-sealing holds across 1000 years, or whether the [[Children of the Hollow Moon]] or other factions have interacted with whatever remains in the Ruidian debris, is an open design question.
