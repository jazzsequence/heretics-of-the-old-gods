# Beauregard Lionett

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Two):** A monk of the [[Cobalt Soul]]'s Expositor program, recruited from a troubled background in Kamordah. Became one of the Mighty Nein and rose to become High Curator of the Cobalt Soul — its highest institutional position — after the War of Ash and Light. Her leadership of the Cobalt Soul during its post-war period included the institutional relationship with the Dwendalian Empire that eventually shaped what the [[Cobalt Veil]] would inherit.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Historical — human lifespan, confirmed dead long before the campaign's present. Relevant as one of the Cobalt Soul's most significant modern-era leaders.

## Personality

Direct, aggressive, protective of people she cares about. Grew significantly across Campaign Two from self-destructive to genuinely principled.
