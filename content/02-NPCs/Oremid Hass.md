# Oremid Hass

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Headmaster of the Hall of Erudition in Zadash, the [[Cerberus Assembly]]'s secondary seat of power in Western Wynandir. A formidable umber hulk-shaped stone giant figure — actually a human who permanently polymorphed himself into this form. Encountered by the Mighty Nein. Operated the Hall of Erudition's functions of domestic magic regulation.

Historical only — no confirmed status post-Campaign Two, presumed dead pre-Exodus.
