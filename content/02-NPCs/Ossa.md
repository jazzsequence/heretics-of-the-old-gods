
# Ossa

## Description

[[Maren Thol]]'s closest ally in the scrapper union. The person most likely to hold the union together after Maren's death if she survives the political chaos. Carrying the weight of knowing that the careful approach just killed her closest friend. Pragmatic enough to work with the PCs even when she doesn't fully trust them.

In Beat 4 of [[Hook B - The Broken Chain]], brings the PCs [[Ren Vassel]] — Maren's contact, a scared low-level Court survey worker who has finally decided staying silent is more dangerous than talking.

## Personality

Pragmatic grief. Doesn't have time to process because the fight didn't pause for Maren's death.
