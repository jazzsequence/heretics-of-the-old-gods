
# Delilah Briarwood

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign One):** Alongside her husband Sylas, Lady Delilah Briarwood was one of the most dangerous mages in Tal'Dorei's history. Originally a member of the [[Cerberus Assembly]], she made a pact with [[Vecna]] in exchange for saving her husband's life, becoming a necromancer of significant power and a loyal servant of the Whispered One. Killed by Vox Machina during the Whitestone arc, then resurrected by Vecna, then killed again by Vox Machina at Entropis during Vecna's ascension attempt.

Historical only — confirmed dead pre-Exodus.
