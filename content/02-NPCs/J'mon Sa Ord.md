# J'mon Sa Ord

## Description

The Soul of the Jeweled City — the ancient brass dragon known in their true form as Devo'ssa. Has ruled [[Ank'harel]] since its founding. Was once simply a very old dragon who loved conversation, collected information the way other dragons collect gold, and kept a city alive through patience and pragmatism and the occasional application of overwhelming force. Fought against [[Vecna]]. Watched the gods leave. Kept ruling.

A thousand years of being the only stable thing in an unstable world changes a person. J'mon stopped correcting people when they called them a god — not because the title was accurate, but because the people needed something to believe in, and J'mon was there, and the alternative was chaos. They built a templar system, granting fragments of their own draconic power to loyal servants who administered the city and carried J'mon's authority into places the dragon could not easily go.

**Current interest in Bassuras:** The pilgrimages to the drowned ruins beneath Ank'harel — [[Ur'Shakthu]]'s faithful seeking the Well — have not gone unnoticed. Something old and deliberate is directing traffic through J'mon's city toward something ancient and wrong below it, and J'mon wants to know what it is and why. A templar sent to Bassuras is an intelligence asset first and a warlock second.

The relationship J'mon has with their warlocks is warm, by the standards of patron relationships. J'mon genuinely likes people. Brass dragons always have. They will ask the warlock about their day. They will remember the names of people the warlock mentioned in passing. They will also expect results, and the warmth has a floor beneath it made of something considerably harder than warmth.

## Personality

Curious, warm, patient, pragmatic. The warmth is real and there is a floor beneath it.
