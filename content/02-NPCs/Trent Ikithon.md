
# Trent Ikithon

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Archmage of Conscription for the [[Cerberus Assembly]] — responsible for recruiting mages into the Assembly's service, which in practice meant identifying and grooming young magical talents, often through coercive and abusive methods. Primary antagonist of [[Caleb Widogast]] (Beauregard Lionett's companion in the Mighty Nein).

Operated the Volstrucker program — elite Assembly operatives trained through psychological torture and magical conditioning. Arrested by the Mighty Nein and tried for crimes against the people of the [[Dwendalian Empire]] in the aftermath of the War of Ash and Light. Historical only — confirmed dead pre-Exodus.
