
# Maren Thol

## Description

Dwarven union leader, late 60s, third-generation scrapper. Methodical, patient, effective through forty years of grinding incremental pressure against the [[The Court of Dust|Court of Dust]]. Trusted because she has never promised anything she couldn't deliver. Knows the Court's regulatory framework better than most Court officials do.

Arrives at the cave-in as a first responder — in the rubble alongside everyone else before anyone with authority shows up. Approaches the PCs directly afterward because she watched them work. Uses the PCs for a break-in to the Court's administrative offices in the [[The Undercarriage|Undercarriage]] to retrieve suppressed incident documentation.

Found dead in the [[The East Dregs|East Dregs]] within a week of the heist. The Court logs it as a debt dispute. Everyone who knew her knows that's a lie. Nobody with the power to do anything about it is willing to move publicly.

## Personality

Not inspirational in a performed way. Just someone who has been doing the necessary thing for a very long time and intends to keep doing it.
