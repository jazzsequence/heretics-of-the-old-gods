
# Maren Thol

## Description

Dwarven union leader, late 60s, third-generation scrapper. Methodical, patient, effective through forty years of grinding incremental pressure against the [[The Court of Dust|Court of Dust]]. Trusted because she has never promised anything she couldn't deliver. Knows the Court's regulatory framework better than most Court officials do.

## Personality

Not inspirational in a performed way. Just someone who has been doing the necessary thing for a very long time and intends to keep doing it.
