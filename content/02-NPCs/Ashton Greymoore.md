
# Ashton Greymoore

## Description

Known to every citizen of Bassuras as the Sanctash, a patron saint of the city embodying the spirit of rebelliousness and vindication. A shrine exists at the former site of the Greymoore Home for Untethered Children — fully covered in graffiti — and symbolic graffiti referencing Ashton's "punk energy" appears throughout the city.

[[The Court of Dust]] tolerates the myth because suppressing it would enrage the city, and instead manages it by reshaping and altering the narrative over time. They understand the danger of letting the oppressed believe too strongly in Ashton's story as a reason they can and should rise up against the Court — but they cannot fully control the effect the story has on people.

**Not established in the source material beyond this:** specific biographical details (dates, deeds, cause of death, connection to the Greymoore Home beyond the shrine's location) are not documented. Treat as a deliberately mythic, lightly-sketched folk figure rather than a fully biographied historical person unless you decide otherwise.
