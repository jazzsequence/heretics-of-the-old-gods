# Imogen Temult

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Three):** A sorcerer with innate psychic abilities and a connection to [[Ruidus]] that intensified as the campaign progressed. A founding member of Bells Hells. Her arc across Campaign Three was deeply tied to [[Predathos]] — her abilities were Ruidian in origin, and she was one of the central figures in the final confrontation at the Apogee Solstice and the subsequent sealing of Predathos.

Notably, at the 843 PD meeting, Imogen specifically advised against allowing [[Vecna]] to take on a mortal form, on the grounds that Predathos could not consume him in a mortal state — a strategic consideration that shaped the Exodus period's conditions.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Historical — human-adjacent lifespan. Her actions at the Apogee Solstice and surrounding events are foundational to the setting's origin.
