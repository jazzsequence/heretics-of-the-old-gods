
# Pol

## Description

Elderly Bassuran, seventies, in the [[The Luxon|Luxon]] mission house care ward for his final weeks. Sharp mind, failing body. Alert in the way people get when the body is failing but the mind isn't.

As a child of about ten years old, followed older kids into a section of the ruins through an access point that has since been sealed or built over. They got further than anyone expected. What they found wasn't cramped buried tunnels — it was a courtyard. Open, vast, architecturally intentional, with the remnants of public structures. The scale was wrong for anything that should be under [[Bassuras]]. None of the other kids talked about it afterward. Adults didn't believe them. Pol eventually stopped trying.

Shares his story with the PCs in pieces across multiple visits, not as a dramatic revelation but as the kind of thing an old man shares when he finally has an audience. He's not sure why it matters. He just knows he saw it and nobody believed him and he'd like someone to know before he's gone.

Dies quietly shortly after [[Auris Vel]] extracts the location details from him. His death is handled with the gentle efficiency the mission house brings to everything. **The courtyard he described is the third access point into the ruins beneath Bassuras** — the one neither the Court nor the Luxon has formally located.

## Personality

Open curiosity with strangers. Asks whether the PCs have ever been underground as the first thing he says to them.
