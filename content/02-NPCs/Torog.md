
# Torog

## Description

A Betrayer God — the Crawling King, god of imprisonment, torture, and the Underdark. One of the most feared of the Betrayer Gods during the age of the Calamity. Known for endless tunneling through the earth and for the imprisonment and prolonged suffering of mortals.

**Relevance to HotOG:** Torog fought [[Erathis]] directly during the Calamity, using [[Ozana]] on [[Issylra]] as their battlefield — collateral damage that killed the civilian population, including [[Ludinus Da'leth]]'s parents. This battle is the founding trauma that drives Ludinus's anti-divine ideology and, by chain of causation, the entire Ruby Vanguard arc and the conditions of the Exodus.

**Status:** Killed during the Calamity. The Betrayer Gods were either destroyed or sealed alongside the Prime Deities behind the Divine Gate.
