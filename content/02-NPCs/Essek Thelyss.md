
# Essek Thelyss

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Shadowhand of the Kryn Dynasty under Leylas Kryn — a title granted to those who focus on the dark mysteries of Exandria for the Bright Queen, encompassing subterfuge, arcana, and investigation. A drow prodigy in dunamancy, only in the second century of his first life and not yet consecuted as of Campaign Two (approximately 835-836 PD). Son of Deirta Thelyss, leader of Den Thelyss.

Known to the Mighty Nein as a complex figure — formally neutral but personally invested in their activities. His theft of two Luxon beacons on behalf of the Cerberus Assembly, which he later revealed to the Mighty Nein, was the inciting act of the War of Ash and Light. Eventually reformed and worked toward making amends.

**Relevance to Heretics of the Old Gods:** Named in [[Quana Kryn]]'s secrets as the person Quana worked with to investigate the true nature of Caelestis's parentage — specifically, the revelation that Caelestis was conceived through Graz'zt's assault on Quana. His status in 1000 A.E. is not established — he was not consecuted as of the pre-Exodus period, and his fate after the Exodus is an open question.

## Personality

Precise, reserved, genuinely invested in understanding dunamantic theory. Morally complicated — capable of significant harm and significant growth.
