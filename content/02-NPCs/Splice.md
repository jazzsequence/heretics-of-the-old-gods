
# Splice

## Description

**Full name:** Cynda Farrow. Half-elf, mid-40s. Fourteen completed Deathwish Runs — more than almost anyone alive in the syndicate. Stubbornly practical, allergic to being managed, deeply curious about things she doesn't understand, which is rare in a city that treats curiosity as a liability.

During the most recent Run, her instruments locked onto anomalous readings near a specific stretch of the Flats and she followed them the way a compass finds north. Found a fissure, dropped in, triggered an Aeorian-era stasis trap in the outer chamber of something vast and ancient buried beneath [[Hellcatch Valley|the Hellcatch]]. Frozen there for weeks, unaware that any time passed. Found and retrieved by the PCs.

Comes out of stasis disoriented. Doesn't fully process how much time has passed until someone tells her the Run season was cancelled. Difficult and combative with anyone trying to keep her in bed, increasingly insistent about going back down. She knows what she saw through the inner door before the trap took her and she intends to reach it.

Her attachment to the tablet isn't greed. She wants to know what it means for [[Bassuras]]. She is very likely to die for that conviction.

## Personality

Doesn't ask permission. Informs people of what she's going to do. Takes care of herself and expects others to take care of themselves.
