
# Caleb Widogast

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Two):** A wizard and former Volstrucker operative, victim of [[Trent Ikithon]]'s conditioning program. Real name Bren Aldric Ermendrud. Joined the Mighty Nein and was central to the effort to expose and arrest Trent Ikithon. One of the most powerful arcane minds of the Campaign Two era.

His stated goal throughout Campaign Two was to obtain enough power to rewrite the past — specifically to undo the moment Ikithon conditioned him into murdering his own parents. He did not ultimately pursue this; the goal transformed into something more present-focused.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Historical — human lifespan. Relevant as context for understanding Trent Ikithon's crimes.

## Personality

Methodical, self-critical, carries enormous guilt. Deeply loyal to people he chooses to trust.
