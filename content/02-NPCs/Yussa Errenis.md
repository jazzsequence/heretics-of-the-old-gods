# Yussa Errenis

## Description
\n\n**Pre-Exodus canon:** Yussa Errenis was a powerful archmage who operated out of Nicodranas, known to the Mighty Nein as the owner of the Tidepeak tower. He had connections to the Cerberus Assembly without being a formal member, and was deeply invested in extraplanar research. Reclusive but capable of significant arcane power.\n\n**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Per the campaign's Rising Action section, the current ruler of the Dwendalian Empire is Queen [[Suria Dwendal II]], an elderly woman whose title is mostly honorific — the true power of the empire comes through the Cerberus Assembly led by Yussa Errenis. How Yussa has survived across 1000 years, whether through extended life, consecution, or other means, is not established in the source material and is an open design question.\n\nIn the Climax section, a rogue faction within the Cerberus Assembly — likely led by Yussa himself — seizes a platoon of Aeormatons (the [[Sentinels of Avalir]]) to fight against [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]] rather than delivering them to her. After the Consecution War, Yussa is unheard from and the Cerberus Assembly eats itself with infighting and internal strife.\n

## Personality

Reclusive, powerful, primarily motivated by arcane research over political loyalty.
