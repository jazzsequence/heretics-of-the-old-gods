
# Sable

## Description

Human, indeterminate middle age, wiry and weathered. Has guided more authorized Court expeditions through the ruins beneath [[Bassuras]] than anyone currently alive in the survey program. Knows the first several mapped chambers cold — patrol patterns, leyline bleed points, where previous expeditions' data suggests danger concentrates.

Standoffish by default because the PCs look like greenhorns dying on schedule, and Sable has watched too many people die to get attached to the next batch. Answers direct questions with the minimum words necessary and moves on. Genuinely honest once they decide someone is worth being honest with.

Knows what the Court doesn't want known: the authorized access point is one of at least three entry points into the ruins beneath Bassuras. Knows the location of the Flats fissure ([[Hook A - Ghost Run]]) and has deduced the existence of a third access point somewhere in the residential district. Tells the PCs because Sable has decided they're trustworthy enough, and because if something happens to Sable underground, someone should know.

Takes damage near the exit of Beat 4 that isn't immediately obviously fatal and doesn't say so. Whether Sable survives is a genuine question. If they do, they become one of the most valuable NPCs the PCs have.

## Personality

Economical. Not unkind, but has no patience for performance. Warms to competence.
