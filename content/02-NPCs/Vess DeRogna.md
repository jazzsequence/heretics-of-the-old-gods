
# Vess DeRogna

## Description

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Three):** Archmage of Antiquity for the [[Cerberus Assembly]], responsible for the recovery and study of pre-Calamity artifacts and lost arcane knowledge. Served as early liaison to the [[Ruby Vanguard]] before betraying [[Ludinus Da'leth]] — or being betrayed by him. Killed by Imogen Temult and others from Bells Hells at the Tishtan excavation site in Marquet during Campaign Three.

Historical only — confirmed dead pre-Exodus.
