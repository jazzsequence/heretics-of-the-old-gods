
# Wildemount

## Overview

Continent east of Tal'Dorei and the setting for Campaign Two. Divided roughly into [[Western Wynandir]] (dominated by the [[Dwendalian Empire]]) and [[Eastern Wynandir]] (the Krynn Dynasty's territory, formerly the wastes of [[Xhorhas]]). Also includes the [[Menagerie Coast]] to the west, [[Eiselcross]] to the far north, and the [[Shattered Teeth]] archipelago.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The western half has shrunk under Krynn expansion — the Empire now covers only lands west of the [[Eisfus River]]. Eastern Wynandir is fully absorbed into the Krynn Dynasty's territory, now centered on [[Rosohna]].
