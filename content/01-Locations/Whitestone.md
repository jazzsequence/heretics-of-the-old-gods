
# Whitestone

## Overview

A city in northern [[Tal'Dorei]], seat of the de Rolo family. Site of the Briarwood arc in [[Critical Role Campaign One|Campaign One]] — the [[Briarwoods|Briarwood]] family seized and occupied Whitestone for years, using it as the location of their attempt to build a ziggurat to [[Vecna]]. Liberated by [[Vox Machina]].

Home of Percy de Rolo (Percival Fredrickstein Von Musel Klossowski de Rolo III), gunsmith and Vox Machina member. The Whitestone Rifle Corps traces its origins to his tenure.
