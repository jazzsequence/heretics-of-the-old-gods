
# Whitestone

## Overview

A city in northern [[Tal'Dorei]], seat of the de Rolo family. Site of the Briarwood arc in Campaign One — the [[Briarwoods|Briarwood]] family seized and occupied Whitestone for years, using it as the location of their attempt to build a ziggurat to [[Vecna]]. Liberated by [[Vox Machina]].

Home of Percy de Rolo (Percival Fredrickstein Von Musel Klossowski de Rolo III), gunsmith and Vox Machina member. The Whitestone Rifle Corps traces its origins to his tenure.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Whitestone is fiercely independent — it participates in the [[Tal'Dorei Council]] alliance but resists [[Emon]]'s commercial dominance, and has the leverage to do so. The city holds a global monopoly on residuum, the magically refined byproduct of whitestone ore found only in the Alabaster Sierras. Residuum is the most strategically valuable magical material on the continent, and probably the world. Whitestone also developed into the largest manufacturer of firearms globally — a direct legacy of Percy de Rolo's work, expanded over a millennium into an industrial base that no other city-state has been able to replicate. The combination of residuum and firearms manufacturing makes Whitestone indispensable and functionally untouchable. Every major power on Nuxan needs what it produces.

The de Rolo line either still holds power or the Chamber of Whitestone has evolved into a more democratic body over the centuries — this is not yet established.
