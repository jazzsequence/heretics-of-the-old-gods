# Darktow

## Overview

Island fortress and base of operations for the pirate alliance known historically as the Revelry. Seat of [[The Plank King]]'s authority. Located in the Shearing Channel between the Menagerie Coast and the continent's western edge.

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Governed by the Plank King through a code of conduct among pirate captains. [[The Mighty Nein]] had significant dealings here.

**Status in Nuxan:** Still the Plank King's seat after 1000 years. After the Climax, largely retreats here as the depopulated [[Menagerie Coast]] proves ungovernable.
