
# Psionics Academy

## Overview

The institution on the [[Shattered Teeth]] where [[Thesik]], [[Barani]], and [[Kosveret]] first met and studied. The origin point of the Mind Lords — all three were students here before the events that led to their consciousness transfer into obsidian orbs.

Details of the Academy's current state, curriculum, or whether it still functions as an institution are not established in the source material. The civilization of the Shattered Teeth has been "slowly dying for centuries," which may or may not have implications for the Academy's operation.
