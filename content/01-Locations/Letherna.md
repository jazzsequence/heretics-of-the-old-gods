# Letherna

## Overview

The domain of the [[Raven Queen]] within the [[Shadowfell]] — her personal realm and the location from which she governed the transit of souls through the Shadowfell toward their ultimate destinations. Not the Shadowfell itself, which predates and exceeds any single entity's territory, but her established seat within it.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The Raven Queen departed during the Exodus. Letherna as a discrete domain may still exist as a structure within the Shadowfell, but without her presence it is ungoverned — and a thousand years of unsorted soul transit, opportunistic dead accumulating power, and Dark Powers feeding on disoriented souls have substantially changed what the Shadowfell surrounding and including Letherna looks like. Whether Letherna itself persists as a recognizable location or has been absorbed into the chaos around it is not established.
