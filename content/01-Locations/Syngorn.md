# Syngorn

## Overview

Former elven city-state in the Verdant Expanse of southern [[Tal'Dorei]]. Syngorn's defining characteristic was its ability to retreat into the Feywild during times of existential threat — a defensive mechanism the city used during the Calamity and again during the Chroma Conclave's assault in Campaign One.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Gone. The Feywild is sealed — the connection that allowed Syngorn to phase between planes no longer exists. Whether the city was in Exandria or the Feywild at the moment of sealing determines what happened to it, but either way the result is the same: the city has no mechanism to exist as it once did. If it was in Exandria, it stands as an abandoned ruin in the Verdant Expanse, its population dispersed over centuries. If it was in the Feywild, it is sealed away with no way out. No living person has confirmed which is true. The Verdant Expanse itself remains, occupied now primarily by Orroyen-adjacent elven nomadic groups and wildlife.
