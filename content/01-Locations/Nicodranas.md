
# Nicodranas

## Overview

Major port city on the [[Menagerie Coast]]. Home to [[Yussa Errenis]]'s Tidepeak tower (pre-Exodus). Part of the [[Clovis Concord]]'s network of coastal city-states.

**Empty Archive:** The [[The Empty Archive|Empty Archive]] occupies a sunken city beneath the [[Lucidian Ocean]] once part of Nicodranas, accessible only during full Luxon eclipses.
