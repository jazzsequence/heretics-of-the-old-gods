# Xhorhas

## Overview

The eastern wastes of [[Wildemount]], historically home to the [[Kryn Dynasty]] and its capital [[Rosohna]] (formerly Ghor Dranas). Barren, monster-ridden terrain that the Dynasty has governed for centuries. Setting of significant Campaign Two action.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Now the heartland of the expanded [[Kryn Dynasty]] under [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]], which has pushed further west across the Ashkeeper Peaks into Western Wynandir.
