# Terrah

## Overview

Home of the Earth Ashari, located within the Cliffkeep Mountains in [[Tal'Dorei]] — the same mountain range that houses [[Kraghammer]]. Terrah guards the earth rift, a stabilizing influence on the geological volatility of the Cliffkeep range.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The Earth Ashari are among the more stable of the four tribes, their underground settlement relatively insulated from the political upheavals of the surface world. Their proximity to [[Kraghammer]] has produced a centuries-long relationship of mutual wariness — dwarves and Ashari sharing the same mountain range without much warmth but without open conflict. The earth rift remains guarded.
