
# Ruidus

## Overview

The smaller, dark red moon of Exandria. Considered an ill omen by most cultures across Exandrian history. Present in the sky since before recorded history — unlike Catha, the other moon, Ruidus does not follow predictable lunar cycles, appearing randomly and erratically.

**Pre-Exodus canon (Campaign Three):** Ruidus was a prison — a moon-sized cage constructed by the Primordials to contain a fragment of Predathos, the Primordial entity that consumed gods. The [[Ruby Vanguard]] Vanguard's plan under [[Ludinus Da'leth]] involved traveling to Ruidus to free [[Predathos]] and use it to destroy the gods. This ultimately failed, and Predathos was re-sealed. The moon sustained significant structural damage during the events of Campaign Three.
