
# Ozana

## Overview

A city on [[Issylra]], destroyed when [[Erathis]] and the Crawling King ([[Torog]]) fought using it as a battlefield — collateral damage in a divine war. [[Ludinus Da'leth]] grew up here as a child and watched his parents die in the rubble. A priest arrived afterward offering theological comfort for a wound theology caused — the moment Ludinus has identified as the origin of his anti-divine conviction.

**Status in Nuxan:** Long destroyed. Relevant only as the founding trauma of Ludinus's ideology — and therefore the originating cause of the entire [[Ruby Vanguard]] arc, the Apogee Solstice events, and the Exodus itself, in the sense that Ludinus's obsession is directly connected to the chain of events that ended the gods' presence in Exandria.
