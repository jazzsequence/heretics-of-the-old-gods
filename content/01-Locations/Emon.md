
# Emon

## Overview

Capital of [[Tal'Dorei]] and the largest city on the continent. Home base of [[Vox Machina]] during [[Critical Role Campaign One|Campaign One]]. Center of the [[Tal'Dorei Council]]'s governance and the de facto seat of power in the post-republic city-state alliance.

The [[The Clasp|Clasp]] originated here, operating via an extensive network of subterranean tunnels beneath the city alongside legitimate street-level presence.
