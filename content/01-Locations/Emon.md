
# Emon

## Overview

Capital of [[Tal'Dorei]] and the largest city on the continent. Home base of [[Vox Machina]] during Campaign One. Center of the [[Tal'Dorei Council]]'s governance and the de facto seat of power in the post-republic city-state alliance.

The [[The Clasp|Clasp]] originated here, operating via an extensive network of subterranean tunnels beneath the city alongside legitimate street-level presence.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Emon's dominance over the Council alliance is commercial and arcane rather than military or political. It controls the majority of trade routes across the continent and houses the **Emon Arcanum** — the rebuilt successor to the Alabaster Lyceum, now functioning as the continent's primary regulatory and political engine for arcane commerce. Other city-states resent Emon's outsized influence but depend on its trade infrastructure enough to remain in the alliance.

The Council is currently coordinating a continental defense response to [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s [[Consecution War]] expanding toward [[Tal'Dorei]].
