# Beynsfal Plateau

## Overview

A high, heat-blasted plateau in the southernmost reaches of [[Tal'Dorei]], formerly the seat of the [[Iron Authority]] — a hobgoblin supremacist empire that waged perpetual war against the [[Rifenmist Jungle]] and its peoples. The plateau's capital Tz'Arrm was built within and around the fallen armor of the Strife Emperor, whose divine armor was melted down over centuries into the empire's walls, weapons, and infrastructure.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The [[Iron Authority]] collapsed in the immediate aftermath of the Exodus. The clerics of the Strife Emperor who had sustained Iron Emperor Tz'Jarr beyond his natural lifespan through blood magic lost their divine power source. Tz'Jarr died. With no succession infrastructure — positions within the Authority were held by appointment and vacated only by death or promotion — the four royal generals immediately turned on each other. The empire fractured into civil war within a generation and devolved into competing warlord states over the following two centuries.

What remains on the Beynsfal Plateau today is a cluster of fiercely independent hobgoblin micronations, culturally descended from the Authority but without its unified military capacity or divine backing. The ideology of goblinkin supremacy persists as cultural inheritance. Slavery persists in some form in most of these states. None of them have the strength to reconstitute the empire or to resume sustained campaigns against the jungle.

Tz'Arrm still stands, now the seat of whichever micronation controls the plateau's center. The Helm of Strife — the Strife Emperor's fallen divine armor — remains, its metal long since stripped for weapons and walls, the empty shell serving as a palace for whoever currently holds power there.

The [[Rifenmist Jungle]] has not been threatened by a unified military force in over eight hundred years.
