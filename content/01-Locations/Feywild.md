# Feywild

## Overview

A mirror plane reflecting Exandria as a place of wild natural beauty, vibrant magic, and heightened emotional intensity. Governed by powerful archfey. Sealed from the Material Plane as of 1000 A.E. — the Feywild is explicitly cut off in the Heretics of the Old Gods setting, with fey-origin subclasses excluded by default.

**Verdant Covenant concern:** The [[The Verdant Covenant|Verdant Covenant]] has uncovered evidence that the Luxon's "light" may be siphoning planar energy from the Feywild — creating a future ecological catastrophe. Whether this is accurate or an artifact of their research methodology is an open design question.
