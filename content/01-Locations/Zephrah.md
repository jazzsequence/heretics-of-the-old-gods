# Zephrah

## Overview

Home of the Air Ashari, situated atop the Summit Peaks in central [[Tal'Dorei]]. Zephrah was the home of Keyleth, Voice of the Tempest and member of [[Vox Machina]], who served as headmaster of the Air Ashari for an extraordinarily long lifespan due to her druidic nature.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Keyleth is long dead. The Air Ashari persist in reduced numbers, their leadership structure intact but their population a fraction of what it once was. The Summit Peaks settlement remains occupied and the air rift remains guarded. As with all [[The Ashari|Ashari]] tribes, their spiritual practice has shifted from deity-aligned druidism to pure elemental guardianship in the absence of divine authority.
