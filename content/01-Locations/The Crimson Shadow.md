# The Crimson Shadow

## Overview

A shattered lunar fragment orbiting Nuxan — debris from the destruction of [[Ruidus]] during Campaign Three's climax. Base of operations for the [[Children of the Hollow Moon]]. Reachable only via old dunamantic engines and hidden magical portals.
