# Vasselheim

## Overview

The oldest city in Exandria, built before the Calamity. Former seat of the world's great religious orders — the Platinum Sanctuary ([[Bahamut]]), the Raven's Crest ([[Raven Queen]]), the Birthheart ([[Melora]]), the Duskmeadow ([[The Raven Queen]], separate from the Crest), the Cobalt Reserve ([[Ioun]]), and the Trial Forge ([[Kord]]). Survived the Calamity when most of the world was devastated, which forms the basis of its cultural identity as a bastion.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** To [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]], Vasselheim represents the basis of all heresy — the city built for old-god worship that persisted longest after the Exodus. Her single-minded focus on purifying the city is the military overextension that makes her vulnerable on other fronts during the Climax. Followers of the old gods and clerical armies from across [[Issylra]] gather here to resist the [[Consecution War]].
