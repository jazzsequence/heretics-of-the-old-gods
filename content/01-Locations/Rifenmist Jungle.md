
# Rifenmist Jungle

## Overview

A vast, sweltering jungle covering the southern expanse of [[Tal'Dorei]], home to the nomadic Orroyen elves and the hidden heart-city of [[Niirdal-Poc]]. The jungle has historically resisted every attempt at conquest or colonization — the [[Iron Authority]] waged cyclical war against it for centuries without lasting success, and the Orroyen's intimate knowledge of the terrain made sustained incursion effectively impossible.

Legend holds that the jungle will never die so long as even one Orroyen lives within it. Whether this is metaphor or literal magic is unclear.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The collapse of the [[Iron Authority]] following the Exodus removed the only sustained military threat the jungle had faced in recorded history. The Rifenmist has had over eight hundred years of uncontested growth. It is healthier, denser, and more expansive than at any point in recorded history. The Orroyen tribes have flourished in the Authority's absence. Their nomadic structure, trial-based membership, and fierce protectiveness of the jungle remain unchanged.

The [[Kryn Dynasty]]'s missionary expansion has not meaningfully penetrated the Rifenmist. The Orroyen have no interest in the Luxon and the jungle's geography makes Aurora Watch operations logistically punishing.

[[Niirdal-Poc]] remains in its basin at the jungle's heart, sealed and self-sufficient.
