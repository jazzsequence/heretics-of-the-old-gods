# Menagerie Coast

## Overview

The western coastal region of Wildemount, historically governed by the [[Clovis Concord]] — a coalition of independent city-states bound by a trade agreement. Once a bastion of free trade and exotic tourism. Home to [[Nicodranas]], [[Darktow]], and the ruins of the Clovis Concord's port cities.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Constantly under threat from the pirates of [[Darktow]] under [[The Plank King]]'s leadership. The Bright Queen has largely left these lands alone, granting them some amount of independence while focusing expansion efforts from Wynandir. In the Climax, The Plank King raises an undead army to annex the city-states. The annexation succeeds but depopulates the harbors and shipyards, leaving graveyards of port infrastructure. After the war, The Plank King retreats to Darktow and largely leaves the abandoned cities to fend for themselves.
