# Pyrah

## Overview

Home of the Fire Ashari, situated near the Cindergrove volcano in [[Tal'Dorei]]. Pyrah guards the fire rift — historically one of the most volatile and dangerous of the four elemental rifts. The settlement was devastated during Campaign One when Thordak the Cinder King used the fire rift to enter Exandria, destroying much of Pyrah in the process. It was rebuilt following his defeat.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The Fire Ashari persist in the smallest numbers of any of the four tribes — the destruction wrought by Thordak's passage was a wound that never fully healed demographically. The fire rift remains guarded, but Pyrah operates with minimal personnel. The settlement is functional but sparse. Their reduced numbers make them the most vulnerable of the [[The Ashari|Ashari]] tribes to extinction.
