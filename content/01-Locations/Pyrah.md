
# Pyrah

## Overview

Home of the Fire Ashari, situated near the Cindergrove volcano in [[Tal'Dorei]]. Pyrah guards the fire rift — historically one of the most volatile and dangerous of the four elemental rifts. The settlement was devastated during [[Critical Role Campaign One|Campaign One]] when Thordak the Cinder King used the fire rift to enter Exandria, destroying much of Pyrah in the process. It was rebuilt following his defeat.
