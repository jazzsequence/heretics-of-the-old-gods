
# Vesrah

## Overview

Home of the Water Ashari, an island settlement off the Ozmit Sea on the western coast of [[Tal'Dorei]]. Vesrah guards the water rift, which connects to the Elemental Plane of Water. The settlement is accessible only by sea or by those invited by the tribe.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The Water Ashari are the most isolated of the four tribes by geography, which has both insulated them from the Consecution War's reach and limited their ability to recruit or grow. Their numbers have held more steadily than Pyrah but they remain a fraction of their historical population. The water rift is guarded. Vesrah maintains occasional trade contact with [[Menagerie Coast]] city-states but participates in no political alliances.
