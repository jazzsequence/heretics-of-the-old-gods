# Aeor

## Overview

The most technologically and magically advanced civilization in Exandrian history — a flying city-state destroyed by the gods during the Calamity for creating weapons theoretically capable of killing them. Fell from the sky and buried itself in the ice of [[Eiselcross]].

Home of the Verdant Cortex hydroponics lab where [[EON]] was built and operated. Birthplace of the [[Sentinels of Avalir]]. The ruins contain preserved Aeorian-era arcanotech, constructs, traps, and stasis fields — all of which are relevant to the underground ruins beneath [[Bassuras]], which appear to be Aeorian in origin and scale.

The ruins have been partially excavated by expeditions from Wildemount since Campaign Two, with the most significant discoveries occurring in Campaign Three's Eiselcross storyline.
