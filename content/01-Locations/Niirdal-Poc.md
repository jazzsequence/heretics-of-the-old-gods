
# Niirdal-Poc

## Overview
**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Niirdal-Poc still exists and the Tetrarchy still governs it. The Gift that once powered miracles and protected the city is gone — the [[Kryn Dynasty]] recovered the object at the heart of the Gift's power during the period of chaos following the [[Iron Authority]]'s collapse, when the jungle's borders were undefended for the first time in recorded history. What the Qoniirans called the Gift was a Luxon beacon. The Kryn retrieved it. Niirdal-Poc lost the miracles but survived. The city's resilience turned out not to depend on the beacon — it depended on the people. The tetrarchy quietly reframed the loss as a theological lesson consistent with their existing doctrine of balance and impermanence.

The city remains effectively inaccessible to outsiders. Few in the wider world know it exists.
