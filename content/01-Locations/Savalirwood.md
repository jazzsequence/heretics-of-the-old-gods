
# Savalirwood

## Overview

A corrupted forest in Wildemount, surrounding the ruins of [[Molaesmyr|Molaesmyr]]. The corruption was caused by [[Ludinus Da'leth]] in 585 PD when his first attempt at the [[Malleus Key]] mechanism went catastrophically wrong, redirecting the crystal well beneath Molaesmyr and killing thousands of elves. The purple-grey blight spreading through the treeline has persisted for centuries.

**In the Domain of Dread:** The Savalirwood surrounding the Mists of Molaesmyr carries purple-grey spores that drift between trees like slow snow. Travelers who breathe them find themselves following a feeling that never resolves, deeper into the Mists.
