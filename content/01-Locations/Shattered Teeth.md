
# Shattered Teeth

## Overview

A remote archipelago off the coast of Wildemount — most people in the Hellcatch have never heard of it and could not find it on a map. The civilization there does not encourage its people to leave, and almost nobody does. Has been slowly dying for centuries.

## Notable NPCs

[[Thesik]], [[Barani]], [[Kosveret]] — the three Mind Lords, present as obsidian orbs.
