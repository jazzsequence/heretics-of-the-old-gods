
# Shattered Teeth

## Overview

A remote archipelago in Wildemount — most people in the Hellcatch have never heard of it and could not find it on a map. The civilization there does not encourage its people to leave, and almost nobody does. Has been slowly dying for centuries.

Home of the three [[Mind Lords of the Shattered Teeth]] — ancient psionicists who transferred their consciousnesses into obsidian orbs millennia ago. The civilization the Mind Lords maintain is built around and dependent on their continued presence. [[Kethren Duvash]] left the Shattered Teeth with what appeared to be official sanction — a mandate to travel the wider world and eventually return with new ideas. This is what he was told. This is what he told himself.

## Notable NPCs

[[Thesik]], [[Barani]], [[Kosveret]] — the three Mind Lords, present as obsidian orbs.
