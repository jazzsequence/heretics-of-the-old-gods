
# Zadash

## Overview

Second largest city of the [[Dwendalian Empire]], seat of the Tri-Spire district and home of the Hall of Erudition — the [[Cerberus Assembly]]'s primary satellite office outside [[Rexxentrum]], where [[Oremid Hass]] served as Headmaster. Major trade hub and significant population center in Western Wynandir.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Not established. Presumably within the contracted territory of the Dwendalian Empire west of the Eisfus River given its location in Wynandir.
