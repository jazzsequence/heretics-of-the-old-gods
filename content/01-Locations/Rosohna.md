# Rosohna

## Overview

Capital of the [[Kryn Dynasty]], built on the ruins of Ghor Dranas — the former dread citadel of the Betrayer Gods. Umbral magic shades the city during daylight hours, an accommodation for the drow who rebuilt it above and below ground after the Divergence. Home of the [[Lucid Bastion]], seat of [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s authority.
