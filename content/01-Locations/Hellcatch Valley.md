
# Hellcatch Valley

## Overview

The Hellcatch Valley in Marquet has a Mad Max crossed with Dark Sun vibe. Magic is scarce and unpredictable, but a tech hub — such as it is — has formed around [[Bassuras]]. Exiles from other parts of the world who left due to being ostracized for knowledge about, skill with, or interest in ancient arcane tech find a home here. A visual language of welded scrap and jury-rigged arcanotech defines the region.

To the rest of Marquet, the Hellcatch Valley is a dumping ground, a trifle not worth bothering with. To its people, Bassuras is a deliberately ignored oasis and a refuge, even in its desperate cruelty. If it were destroyed, the people would expect it to come back. It always does.

Safe travel, functional tech, and reliable magic are all scarce. Travel through the valley is threatened by raiders who attack caravans out of the dust storms. The desert itself is fatal — the ultimate threat, where people are left to die. Succumbing to the elements (getting lost, starving, running out of water, dust storms) is more likely to kill you than raiders, who are infrequent and generally only strike known trade routes.
