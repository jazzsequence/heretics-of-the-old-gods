# Issylra

## Overview

Continent containing [[Vasselheim]], the oldest city in Exandria and former seat of the gods' faith. Sparsely populated relative to Wildemount and Tal'Dorei, with scattered republics and communities. Home to the Othanzia region where Vasselheim sits.

In the Rising Action of HotOG, followers of the old gods and clerical armies gather in Issylra to resist the [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s [[Consecution War]]. [[Vasselheim]] is the Bright Queen's specific target — to her it represents the basis of all heresy.
