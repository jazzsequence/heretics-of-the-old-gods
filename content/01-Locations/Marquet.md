
# Marquet

## Overview

Continent south of Wildemount, featuring vast deserts, major trade cities, and diverse cultures. Home of [[Ank'harel]], [[Bassuras]], and the [[Hellcatch Valley]]. Campaign Three was set primarily in Marquet.

The [[The Court of Dust|Court of Dust]]'s reach does not extend beyond the Hellcatch to other major Marquesian cities. Those cities are independent but less developed relative to their pre-Exodus state.
