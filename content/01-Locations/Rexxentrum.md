
# Rexxentrum

## Overview

**Pre-Exodus canon:** Capital of the [[Dwendalian Empire]], seat of the [[Cerberus Assembly]]'s power and the location of the Archive of the Knowing Mistress. One of the most powerful cities in Wildemount.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** The once great Dwendalian Empire has shrunk significantly under [[Bright Queen Caelestis Kryn]]'s expansion, now covering only the lands west of the Eisfus River. The [[Cobalt Veil]] cites reconstructing the Archive of the Knowing Mistress buried beneath the ruins of Rexxentrum as their central goal, implying the Archive's destruction or burial in the intervening centuries.
