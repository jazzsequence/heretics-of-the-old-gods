# Cael Morrow

## Overview

The Drowned City — an ancient settlement submerged beneath [[Ank'harel]] and the sands of [[Marquet]]. Predates Ank'harel itself. Contains ruins of significant arcane importance, including deposits of [[Ruidium]] and access to submerged structures connected to the deep Primordial history of Exandria.

[[Ur'Shakthu]]'s most devoted Stormbound pilgrims navigate down into these ruins seeking the Well — the source of Ur'makthi — in what is described as a submerged demiplane of crushing pressure and pervasive sorrow. The exact relationship between Cael Morrow the archaeological ruin and the demiplane the Stormbound describe is not fully mapped in the source material.

**Campaign Three:** Bells Hells investigated Cael Morrow extensively during the Ruidus arc. The ruins contain elements connected to the history of [[Predathos]]'s imprisonment.
