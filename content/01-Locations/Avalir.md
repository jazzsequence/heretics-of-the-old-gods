# Avalir

## Overview

A flying city — one of the sky-cities of [[Aeor]]'s civilization, traveling above Exandria before the [[The Calamity|Calamity]]. Home of the [[Sentinels of Avalir]], Aeormatons built for its defense. Fell and was buried in [[Eiselcross]] during the Calamity.

The broader Aeorian civilization of which Avalir was a part built [[EON]] in the Verdant Cortex of the main Aeor structure. Whether Avalir and the main Aeor ruin are the same structure or adjacent ruins in the same glacial field is not definitively established in the sources — treat as adjacent unless established otherwise.
