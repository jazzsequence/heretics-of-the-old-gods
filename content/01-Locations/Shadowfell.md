# Shadowfell

## Overview

A dark reflection of the Material Plane, existing as a parallel dimension of shadow and death. Home of [[Vecna]]'s undead forces during his rise, and historically regulated by the presence of the [[Raven Queen]] in her realm of Letherna.

**Status in Nuxan (1000 A.E.):** Without the Raven Queen's governance, the Shadowfell's soul-transit infrastructure still functions but nobody is sorting the mail. A thousand years of unsorted soul transit has produced a Shadowfell significantly more dangerous and politically complex than anything the people of Nuxan understand. The ambitious and predatory dead have entrenched themselves as unofficial Darklords. The Dark Powers have been feeding on the influx of disoriented souls. See [[DM Notes]] — The Afterlife Problem for full detail.
