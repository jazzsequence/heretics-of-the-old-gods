# Lucidian Ocean

## Overview

The ocean east of the continent of Wildemount, bordering [[Nicodranas]] and the Menagerie Coast. Home to Uk'otoa, sealed beneath its floor.

**Empty Archive base:** The [[The Empty Archive|Empty Archive]] maintains a base in a sunken city beneath the Lucidian Ocean, once part of [[Nicodranas]], accessible only during full Luxon eclipses.
