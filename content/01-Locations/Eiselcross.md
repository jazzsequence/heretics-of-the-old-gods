
# Eiselcross

## Overview

Frozen wastes north of [[Wildemount]], site of the ruins of the flying city of [[Aeor]] — the most advanced arcane civilization in Exandrian history, destroyed by the gods during the Calamity for constructing weapons capable of killing them. The ruins are buried under glacial ice and partially accessible via excavation.

**Relevance to HotOG:** Archaeologists in Eiselcross uncover an army of Aeormatons in the Climax — the Sentinels of Avalir, created to defend Aeor during the Divergence. The Sentinels' connection to [[EON]] (also an Aeorian-era construct, built in Aeor's [[Verdant Cortex]]) is significant.
