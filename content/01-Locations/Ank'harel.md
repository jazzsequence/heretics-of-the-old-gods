
# Ank'harel

## Overview

Known as the Jewel of Hope. Grand city at the center of the [[Marquet|Marquesian]] desert, built atop the Drowned City of [[Cael Morrow]]. Ruled by the mysteriously enduring [[J'mon Sa Ord]] since its founding. A magnificent haven of music and culture amid the monster-infested sands that surround it.

## Notable NPCs

[[J'mon Sa Ord]] — ruler since founding.
